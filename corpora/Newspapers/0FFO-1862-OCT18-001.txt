COTTON in ASIA MINOR.—The Imperial Ottoman
Government having issued a decree, under date the 28th August, for
the encouragement of cotton cultivation, the Imperial Cotton Commis¬
sion for the province of Smyrna make known that they are desirous of
receiving from manufacturers particulars of GINS, Cleaning Machi¬
nery, and Agricultural Implements suitable for the province. Those
which are to be sent beyond the mountains must not exceed 3cwt.
in one piece. The Commission propose to hold an Exhibition in
Smyrna of cotton products and of implements, for which prizes will be
awarded by the Imperial Government.
Communicatious to be addressed, post paid. Imperial Cotton Com¬
mission, Smyrne.
BOGHOS EFFENDI, Secretary.
Smyrna, Sept. 25, 1862.
