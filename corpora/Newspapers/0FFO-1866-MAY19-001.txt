brand or trade mark of Henry Clay to a large extent, and selling
cigars with such brand or trade mark that are not the manufacture
of the said Messrs. Julian Alvarez and Co., thersby deceiving the
public as well as damaging the reputation of the said brand or trade
mark. Now be it known unto all whom it may concern, that the said
Messrs. Julian Alvarez and Co., of Calle de San Nicolas, No. 128,
Havana, are the only makers of and dealers in cigars known or sold
under the said brand or trade mark of Henry Clay, and that they are
the only persons entitled to make use of such name, brand, or trade
mark in connexion with cigars; and notice is hereby given, that the
undersigned acting on behalf of the said Messrs. Julian Alvarez and
Co., will take immediate proceedings against all persons who shall import
into this country, or manufacture, or in any way deal in cigars under
the said name, brand, or trade mark of Henry Clay that are not of
