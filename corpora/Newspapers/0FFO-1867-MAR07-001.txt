COUNTY of CARNARVON. —Seiont, Gwrial, and
Llyfni Fishery District.—Notice is hereby given, that in pursu¬
ance of Section 5 of the statute 28th and 29th Victoria, cap. 121, that the
Right Hon. Spencer Horatio Walpole, one of Her Majesty's Principal
Secretaries of State, has granted a CERTIFICATE under his hand
dated the 2d day of March, 1867, forming a FISHERY DISTRICT
for the RIVERS SEIONT, Gwrfai, and Llyfni, in the said county of
Carnarvon, and that the following is a copy of such certificate.
W. T. POOLE, Clerk of the Peace.
Carnarvon, March 5th, 1867.
SALMON FISHERY ACT, 1865.
The Limits of the Fishery District of the Rivers Seiont, Gwrfai,
and Llyfni shall be as follows:—
So much of the Rivers Seiont, Gwrfai, and Llyfni, and their tribu¬
taries, as is situate within the county of Carnarven, and so much of
the coast as lies between the eastern side of the Ferry Causeway at
Garth Point, in the parish of Bangor, and the Headland or Point of
Llanaelhaiarn, in Carnarvon Bay, both in the said county of Carnar¬
von, and including half that portion of the Menai Straits to the middle
line, which lies south of the said Ferry Causeway, as laid down on the
Map deposited in the Office of the Clerk of the Peace for the county of
Carnarvon.
I do hereby certify that the Fishery District above described is duly
formed by me.
Given under my hand this 2d day of March, 1867.

gned
S. H. WALPOLE.
