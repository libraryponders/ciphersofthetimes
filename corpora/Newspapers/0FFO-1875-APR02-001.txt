Albert Joseph Edouard Marie Tyrel-de Poix, of No. 3, Argyll-road,
in the parish of Kensington, in the county of Middlesex, Artist, formerly
known as EDMOND ALBERT JOSEPH DURIEUX and subse¬
quently as EDMOND ALBERT JOSEPH DE POIX TYREL intend
henceforth to USE in all deeds and writings, on all occasions, and for
all purposes, the NAMES of EDMOND ALBERT JOSEPH
EDOUARD MARIE TYREL DE POIX, instead of the names herein¬
before mentioned, by which I have been heretofore known (the said
name of Durieux having been assumed at the time of the French Re¬
volution, for political reasons or purposes of non-recognition, by my
grandfather, Pierre Marie De Poix, of the province D'Artois, now Dé¬
partment du Pas de Calais, in France, an Officer in the French Army

though adding instead of prefixing thereto the additional family name
of Tyrel); and that I have by deed poll, under my hand and seal, and
duly enroiled in the High Court or Chancery, assumed the names of
Edmond Albert Joseph Edouard Marle Tyrel de Poix; and I do
hereby authorize and desire every person whomsoever to designate and
describe ma by such names accordingly.—Dated this 25th day of
