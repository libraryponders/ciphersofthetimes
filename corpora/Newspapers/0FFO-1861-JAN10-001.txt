GREENWICH HOSPITAL SCHOOIS.
THE Lords Commissioners of the Admiralty have
been pleased to direct that admission to this School by patronage
or by any exclusive right of commissioned officers of the Royal Navy
shall cease from and after the 31st December, 1860, with the exception
of the right of nomination thereto hitherto accorded to Lloyds (Pa¬
triotie Fund).
The claims of candidates will from the above mentioned date, be in¬
vestigated by a Committee of Selection at Greenwich Hospital, and all
applications for admission to the School must be addressed to the Secre¬
tary of Greenwich Hospital, who will furnish the applicants with all in¬
formation as to the necessary qualifications.
By order of the Commissioners of Greenwich Hospital,
J. W. NICHOLLS, Sec.

NOTICE to the HOLDERS of UNEXPIRED REMITTANCE
CERTIFICATES for PASSAGES to NEW SOUTH WALES.
THE Holders of Passage Remittance Certificates,
issued by the Colonial Government of New South Wales since the
1st January, 1860, who have not yet communicated with the Emigration
Board, are requested forthwith to SEND IN their CERTIFICATES,
stating at the same time whether they wish to emigrate or not, in
order that steps may be taken either to provide passages for those who
wish to emigrate, or to enable the depositors in the colony to obtain a
return of their deposits in the case of those who do not wish to emi¬
grate.
By order of the Board,
JOHN WALPOLE, Assistant Secretary.
