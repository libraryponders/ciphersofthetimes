of principal dimensions, rig, register tonnage and tonnage per
Thames measurement, name of builder, date of build, name of
owner, and port belonging to; and in cases where the vessels are
classed by this Society, the character will be inserted.

building of iron, wood, and composite yachts intended for classifica¬
