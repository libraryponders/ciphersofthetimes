CHARITY COMMISSION.—Inthe Matter of the
Charity called The ROSPITAL of ST. CROSS, near Winchester,
and In the Matter of "The Charitable Trusts Acts, 1853 to 1869."—
By direction of the Board of Charity Commissioners for England and
Wales, notice is hereby given, that an ORDER is proposed to be
made by them after the expiration of one calendar month, to be com¬
puted from the first publication of this notice, ESTABLISHING a
NEW SCHEME, in partial variation of the existing scheme, for the
REGULATION of the above mentioned CHARITY. A printed
draft of the proposed scheme may be inspected, free of cost, on each
week day, during a period of 15 days from the first publication of the
notice, at the Hospital of St. Cross, between the hours of 11 a.m. and
3 p.m., and at the oince of the said Commissioners, Whiteball, Lon¬
don, S.W., between the hours of 10 a.m. and 4 p.m. Any objections to
the proposed Order, or suggestions for its modification, may be
transmitted, in writing, to the Secretary of the said Commissioners,
Whitehall, London, S.W., within 21 days next after the first publica¬
tion of this notice.—Dated this 18th day of April. 1878.
HENRY M. VANE, Secretary.
