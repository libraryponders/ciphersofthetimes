BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Stock.—In
pursuance of an Order of the Court of Chancery, dated the 21st
July, 1865, and made in the matter of the estate of Esther Peto,
deceased, &c., the Officers of the Bank of England are directed to re¬
transfer from the Commissioners for the Reduction of the National
Debt the sum of £1,538 6s. Consolidated £3 per Cent. Annuities, here¬
tofore standing in the name of CHARLES ELEY, of Beaver's Farm,
near Hounslow, Middlesex, farmer, and which was transferred to the
said Commissioners in consequence of the dividends thereon not having
been received since the 5th January, 1855;—Notice is hereby given that,
on the expiration of three months from this date, the said Stock will
be Transferred, and the Dividends thereon paid, to Charles Eley
the person named in the said order, unless some other claimant shall
sooner appear and make out his claim thereto.
