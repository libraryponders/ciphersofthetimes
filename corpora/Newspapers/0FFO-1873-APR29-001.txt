BE JOSEPH J. de LIZARDI.—Withdrawal of Re¬
ward for Apprehension.—The undersigned having been com
peled to pay up the sum of £12,000 as the penalty for the non-appear¬
ance of the abovenamed, hereby WITHDRAW the £200 REWARD
offered by them for the apprehension of the abovenamed Joseph J. de
Lizardi. In making this announcement they consider it due to them¬
selves to state that the withdrawal of the reward is not from any de¬
sire that he should not be brought to justice, but they decline to incur
further expense in consequence of our application to extend the time
for his production having been refused. —Dated April 24th, 1873.
JOHN HEGAN.
