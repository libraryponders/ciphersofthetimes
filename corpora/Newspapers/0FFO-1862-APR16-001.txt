To the SHAREHOLDERS of the CRYSTAL PALACE COMPANY.

Report having, "by some unaccountable means" got
into some of the newspapers that I have resigned my seat at the
Crystal Palace Board, I take the earliest opportunity of informing you
that it is untrue ; that I continue, as heretofore, to look after your
interests, which are identical with my own, being myself one of the
largest holders in the Company, with my mind thoroughly devoted to
its interest. I remain, Gentlemer
Your faithful servant.
Hatcham, April 14, 1862.
