NOTICE is hereby given, that an APPLICATION
has been made to the Board of Trade by the Bradwell Oyster
Fishery Company (Limited), whose registered offices are situate at
Silver-street, Maldon, in the County of Essex, for a GRANT to them
their successors, and assigns, under "The Sea Fisheries Act. 1868.
of a several OYSTER or MUSSEL FISHERY in BRADWELL¬
CREEK and PEWIT ISLAND, adjacent to or in the parish of Brad¬
well-juxta-Mare, in the County of Essex; and also in part of the bed
of the river Backwater, adjacent to or in the said parish of Bradwell¬
juxta-Mare.
The proposed Fishery is described as follows:
All that part of the bed of the Bradwell Creek, in the parish of
Bradwell-juxta-Mare, containing an area of 15 seres or thereabouts,
which is covered with water at low neap tides (except any such parts
of such creek as may belong to private owners). Also all that part of
the bed of the River Blackwater, in the parish of Bradwell-juxta¬
Mare, which is covered by water at a low neap tide, containing an
area of 100 acres or thereabouts, and bounded, as follows—that is to
say, at one end thereof by an imaginary line drawn from south to
north, from the Coastguard Flagstaff at Bradwell Waterside, in the
direction of Great Wigborough Church; at the other end thereof by
an imaginary line drawn from south to north, from the farmhouse in
Waymarks farm, in the parish of Bradwell-juxta-Mare, in the direc¬
tion of West Mersea Church; on one side thereof by the line of low
water at nea tides on the Bradwell side of the river Blackwater;
and on the other side thereof by an imaginary curved line drawn
parallel with the last-mentioned fine at an average of one-eighth of a
mile to the north of the said last-mentioned line. Also the whole of
the Pewit Island, in the parish of Bradwell-juxta-Mare, containing an
area of 26 acres or thereabouts
The Order applied for as aforesaid will, if granted, confer on the
undertakers a right of several oyster or mussel fishery within the
above limits. The said Order will also specify the manner in which
the limits of the fishery will be marked out, and the manner in which
notice of the said limits may be given to fishermen, dredgermen, and
others, in event of such marks becoming obliterated by unavoidable
accident. It will also require the undertakers to render accounts to
the Board of Trade, and provide for the inspection of their books and
documents by the Board of Trade.
It is proposed that the said Order shall continue in operation for 60
years from its confirmation by Act of Parlament.
The Draft Order can be seen at the office of the undertakers, situate
at Silver-street, Maldon, in the county of Essex, for one caiendar
month from the date of this adverusement, between the hours
of 11 in the forenoon and 3 in the afternoon; and copies thereof may
pe obtained at the place and times aforesaid, at the price of one
shilling each.
Copies of the Draft Order and of the plans of the aforesaid proposed
fishery, deposited

charge) at the office of the undertakers at Silver-street, Maldon, in
the county of Essex; at the Custom-nouse at Maldon aforesaid; and

Written objections to the grant of the proposed Order will be re¬
