FIRE in the BRIXTON-ROAD.—In reply to nu¬
merous enquiries, Mr. C. W. ANDREW (late Tye and Andrew),
builder and sanitary engineer, Brixton-road, desires to inform his
friends and patrons that he has rebuilt his workshops. The tempor¬
ary inconvenience which the late calamitous fire occasioned is over¬
come. Business is now carried on without interruption. Most fortu¬
nately his large stock of patent sink traps, so much in demand at this
season of the year, escaped damage, and he is still executing shipping
orders with his usual promptness.
