England, about A.D. 1755, and moved to Detroit, Michigan,
about A.D. 1796, and to Jeffersonville, Indiana, in 1816, where he died
in 1825. He had, while in the United States, a wife, whose maiden
name was Wilson She died in 1822, never having had any children Early
in 1823 Maria York Harvey, a reputed daughter of John Harvey, came
from England to Jeffersonville, Indiana, and died there in 1826 or 27.
She was married to Edwin Reeder, May 18, 1826, but never had any
children. She was born in England about A.D. 1796, and it was said
she had two older sisters living in some part of England. For any evi¬

the residence of either of the above parties in England a

heirs of John Harvey and Maria York Harvey a Reward of Ten

United States Army in the late war. Detroit. Michigan, United States.
