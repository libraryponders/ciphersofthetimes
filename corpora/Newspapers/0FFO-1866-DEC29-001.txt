of Salvage has been instituted in the High Court of Admiralty of
England, on behalf of the owners, master, and crew of the fishing
smack Welfare against the schooner or vessel JANE CAMERON, her
tackle, apparel and furniture, and the cargo now or lately laden
therein, together with the freight due for the transportation thereof;
and whereas a portion of the said cargo, consisting of gunpowder, is
now lying in Lowestoft Harbour, under arrest, by virtue of a warrant

to give notice to all persons who have, or claim to have, any RIGHT,
title, or interest in the said GUNPOWDER, that if an appearance in
the said cause be not entered in the Registry of the said Court within
six days from the publication of this notice, the Judge of the said
Court will order the said gunpowder to be sold to answer the claims
instituted or to be instituted against the same, or make such order
in the premises as to him shall seem right. —Dated the 27th day of
December, 1866.
