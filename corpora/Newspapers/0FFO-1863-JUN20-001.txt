MAIZENA.—It has been recently discovered that a
person has been manufacturing a spurious and inferior com¬
pound, which has been sold with the name and title of Maizena affixed
thereto. The Glen Cove Company, of New York, are the only manu¬
facturers of "a peculiar preparation of the most nutritious properties
of the Indian corn, which preparation the said Company have for some
years sold under the title of Maizena, which word forms an essential
part of their trade mark. The Glen Cove Company hereby CAUTION
all persons from infringing their said trade mark, and from using the
name of Maizena, or any colourable imitation thereof, as in case of
breach immediate proceedings will be taken in the Court of Chancery
for an injunction. The Glen Cove Company also give notice, that the
sole consignees of Maizena in the United Kingdom are Messrs. Tomlin,
Rendell, and Co., 33, Eastcheap, London, whose names are affixed
to every pack et.
