January next, at 10 o'clock precisely, at the Sessions-house, on Clerken¬

adjournment at the said Sessions-house, on Thursday, the 15th day of
the same month of January, on which last-mentioned day, at 12 o'clock.

den, and so much of the parish of Harrow on-the-Hill as is not within
the district of the Harrow Local Board of Health, constituted under

the 25th and 26th Victoria, cap. 61. —Dated this 30th day of December,
1862.
