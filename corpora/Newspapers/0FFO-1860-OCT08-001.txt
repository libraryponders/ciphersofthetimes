literary institution. The club department is now conducted with great
satistaction to the members, by Mr. T. Rudkin. The club-house has
recently been rebuitt, and possesses all the necessary room for the ac¬
conmofation of 1,500 members. In the large room, one of the hand¬

and forms of nomination may be had at the Club-house.
