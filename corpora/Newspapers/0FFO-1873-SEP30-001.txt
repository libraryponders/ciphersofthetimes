PORT of LONDON.—The Corporation of London,
as the Sanitary Authority of the Port of London, hereby requests
agents, owners, and commanders of all vessels arriving in the Thames
from Russian, German, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Belgian, French,
and other ports with emigrants on board, to give early intimation to
Dr. Harry Leach, the Menical Officer of Health, as to such arrival in
order that the EMIGRANTS may be INSPECTED by him before the
vessel hauls into dock. Such intimation to be addressed to the Port
of London Sanitary office, Deptford.
