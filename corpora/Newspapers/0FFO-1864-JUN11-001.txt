BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividends.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of Two Dividends on the sum of
£1,213 6s. 9d. Consolidated Three per Cent. Annuities heretofore
standing in the name of Hon ELIZABETH SUSAN WILLOUGHBY.
of The Terrace, Piccadilly, spinster, deceased, and which Dividends
were paid over to the Commissioners for the Reduction of the National
Debt, in consequence of the first thereof having remained unclaimed
since the 5th January, 1854; notice is hereby given, that on the ex¬
piration of three months from this date, the said Dividends will be
paid to the Hon. Alberic Drummond Willoughby, the sole executor,
who has claimed the same, unless some other claimant shall sooner
appear and make out his claim thereto.
