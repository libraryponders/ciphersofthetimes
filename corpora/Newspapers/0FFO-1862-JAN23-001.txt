BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividend.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of one dividend on the sum of
£2,896 10s. 7d. £3 5s. per Cent. Annuities, heretofore standing in the
name of EDWIN TILSLEY, of Moreton-in-the-Marsh, county of Glo¬
cester, gentleman, and which dividend was paid over to the Commis¬
sioners for the Reduction of the National Debt in consequence of its
having remained unclaimed since the 10th October, 1851;—Notice is
hereby given, that, on the expiration of three months from this date,
the said Dividend will be paid to the said Edwin Tilsley, who has
claimed the same, unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and
make out his claim thereto.
