BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividend.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of one dividend on the sum of
£1,579 4s. 8d. Consolidated 3 per Cent. Annuities, heretofore stand¬
ing in the name of OCTAVIUS TRAVERS, of Madras, East Indies,
Esq., and which dividend was paid over to the Commissioners for the
Reduction of the National Debt, in consequence of its having re¬
mained unclaimed since the 5th July, 1852;—Notice is hereby given
that, on the expiration of three months from this date the said dividend
will be paid to the said Octavius Travers, who has claimed the same,
unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and make out his claim
thereto.
