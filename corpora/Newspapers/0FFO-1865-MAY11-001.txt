England to direct the payment of one dividend on the sum of
£1,632 11s. 9d. Consolidated £3 per Cent. Annuities, heretofore stand¬
ing in the names of JAMES WARE, of Bridge-street, Blackfriars,
sq., EBENEZER, MAITLAND, of Coleman-street, Esq., and
ROBERT GRANT, of Russell-square, Esq., all deceased, and which
dividend was paid over to the Commissioners for the reduction of the
National Debt, in consequence of its having remained unclaimed since
the 5th January, 1828; Notice is hereby given that, on the expiration
of three months from this date, the said Dividend will be paid to
Margaret Percy wife of Lord Josceline William Percy (formerly Mar¬
garet Grant, widow), the administratrix of Sir Robert Grant, deceased
(formerly Robert Grant, Esq.), who was the survivor, who has claimed
the same, unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and make
out his claim thereto.
