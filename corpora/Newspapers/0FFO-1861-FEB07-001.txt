EDICT.—The Imp. Provincial Tribunal of Trieste
does hereby notify, that Authony Werk, quondam Felix, by his
petition exhibited under date of the 4th inst. (June), No. 5,204, requested
the declaration of demise of Francis Urban Werk, quondam Felix, born
on the 1st of February, 1798, at Britof, in the Empire of Austria, who
in 1827 left direct for London, from whence nothing has been heard of
him since 1829 up to this day; in deputing the advocate Dr. Denipoti
as guardian or curator of the said absent FRANCIS URBAN WERK,
the said Court SUMMONS the latter at the same time to appear
within a year before this tribunal, or else to authenticate his existence,
so much more as in the contrary proceedings would be entered into for
the declaration of his decease, and his property adjudicated and
handed over to that or those persons who will be lawfully entitled to it,
and particularly that part of it which is devolved to him in consequence
of the death of Cecilia Lucia Werk, widow of the late Francis Millauz.
Trieste, the 27th June, 1860.
