appointment, dated the 3d of March, 1859, bequeathed all the residue
of the money to arise from her real and personal estates unto and
equally among all her first cousins living at the time of her decease
(whether on the part of her late father, George Pickard, formerly of
New Millerdam, in the parish of Sandal Magna, in the county of
York, or of her late mother, Catherine Pickard, before her marriage
called Catherine Hughes, and residing near Holywell, in the county of
Flint) who shall claim the same, or their respective shares thereof, or
should be otherwise ascertained within 24 calendar months next after
her decease, in total exclusion of any of such first cousins who should
not claim or be ascertained within the period aforesoid; Notice is
hereby given, that every person claiming to be one of such FIRST
COUSINS of the said MARTHA FREEMAN as aforesaid, must
within the period aforesaid send in such CLAIM, with the evidence
in support thereof, to Mr. James Whitham, solicitor, Wakefield, the
solicitor for the surviving and acting executors under the said will,
otherwise he or she will be excluded from participating in any benefit
under the said bequest. — Dated the 26th day of July, 1862.
