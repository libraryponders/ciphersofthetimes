To the NEXT of KIN, if any, and all other persons having, or
claiming to have, any right or title or interest in or to the personal
estate of JAMES BENJAMIN HILL ALBUOY, late of Bury-street,
in the parish of St. James's, Westminster, in the county of Middlesex,
a Captain in her Majesty's 21st Regiment of Royal North British
Fusileers, deceased (who died near Brighton, in the county of Sussex, on
the 5th day of November, 1852, intestate).—Take notice, that a CITA¬
TION, bearing date the 5th day of June, 1861, was issued, under the
Seal of Her Majesty's Court of Probate, at the instance of John
Marmaduke Teesdale, of Fenchurch-Street, London, a creditor of the

thereof, to cause an appearance to be entered for you in the Principal
Registry of the said Court, with an intimation that, in default of your
appearing and accepting and extracting such letters of administra¬

said John Marmaduke Teesdale, the Judge of the said Court, or the
Registrars of the Principal Registry thereof, will proceed to grant
letters of administration of such personal estate and effects of the said
James Benjamin Hill Albuoy, to the said John Marmaduke Teesdale
your absence notwithstanding. —Dated this 20th day of June, 1861.
H. L. STRONG. Registrar.
