MURDER.—FOUND, on 8th inst., in a cupboard
in No. 11 Room. Hotel Victoria, Rouen, the BODY of a
MALE CHILD, about four years of age. The information obtained
showt that the child was murdered on the night of 13th May last by
a woman who called herself Marie Rhoda Anderson, and who arrived
at Dieppe on that day from Newhaven, and left the Hotel Victoria in
the evening of the 14th. Description of Woman.—Age 25 to 30, very
tall and thin, masculine countenance, complexion swarthy, hair fair
(worn in plain bands), speaks French with slight English or fherman
accent, dressed in black tight-fitting jacket, black silk or stuff dress,
waterproof cloak (English shaps), round black straw hat, spotted veil;
supposed t be an English lady's-maid. Description of Child.—Age
four to five years, hair fair; dress—round straw hat, turned up brims,
trimmed with brack riband, woollen frock trimmed with black galloon,
pelt of same material, coarse stuff under petticoat (black and brown
stripes), woollen jacket trimmed with white braid, gray stockiags,
side-spring boots (English make) shirt without sieeves. Information
