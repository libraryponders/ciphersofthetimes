PADDINGTON WORKHOUSE. — Workhouse Nurse
(Female).—There is a VACANCY for a NURSE at the In¬
firmary of the Paddington Workhouse, for which the Board of Guar¬
dians invite applications from candidates, under 40 years of age, who
have had some hospital training or experience in some institution.
Salary £20 per annum, rising £2 annually to £26, with board, lodging,
washing, and uniform. The appointment will be made subject to the
approval of the Local Government Soard. Applications, accompanied
by testimonials of recent date (not exceeding three in number), will
be received at the Workhouse until the morning of Wednesday, the
29th instant, on which day, at 10 o'clock in the forenoon, the Guar¬
dians will consider the applications of such candidates as may then
and there be in attendance.
