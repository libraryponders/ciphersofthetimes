Merchants, Shipowners, Commanders and Officers of Ships, and all
interested in the improvement of sailors, are hereby requested to meet
in the London Tavern, at noon, THIS DAY. June 5.
Sir JAMES DUKE, Bart., will take the chair.

TOYNBEE, F.R.A.S., deduced from his experience of nearly 30 years
spent among sailors.

prominent features are:—A home for the wives and families of seamen,
and a fund, to which every sailor may subscribe, for his support in old
