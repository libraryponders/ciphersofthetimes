defendant in the above suit, containing a statement that the motion
to restrain him from manufacturing paraffine oil, in infringement o
Mr. Young's patent, has been refused, the public are hereby informed

the suit is heard, and that meanwhile the defendant has been ordered

selling any paraffine oil, made according to Mr. Young's patent, which
