ANGHAM HOTEL.—Appointment of Chef
Housekeeper.—The Directors of the Langham Hotel Company
(Limited) are prepared to receive APPLICATIONS, in writing, for
the POSITION of CHIEF HOUSEKEEPER at the establishment of
the Company, situate in Portland-place, London.
The duties will consist of the general charge of all female and
domestic service throughout the hotel and steam laundry (exclusive
of the kitchen so far as regards all culinary operations under the
chei-do-cuisine.
The Directors desire the aid of a resident lady to receive the guests
upon arrival, to ascertain, by personal visit and attention, that their
comfort and wishes are studied during their stay at the hotel, and to
take leave of them upon departure.
The office of chief housekeeper is one of good position for a lady.
acting under the immediate authority of the Board of Directors, and
her duties will be lightened by the assistance of departmental second,
third, and fourth housekeepers, of considerable experience in the
service of the Company.
The salary will be at the rate of £80 per annum for the first six
months, £90 per annum for the second six months, and £100 per
annum after 12 monthis' ongagement; and a comfortable home, with
liberal board and arrangements, are provided by the Company in ad¬
dition to the free laundry and medical attendance.
Applications must be made (in writing) within all June instant,
under cover, with wax seal, addressed to the Board of Directors, Lang¬
ham Hotel, Portland-place, London, and must state the age of the
applicant and previous occupation (if any), accompanied by testi¬
monials. It is essential that the lady should be of British parentage,
and that English shall be her native tongue, but knowledge of French
or any foreign language will command attention.
It is optional to send in photograph or carte de visite, and all com¬
munications will be treated as strictly confidontial.
