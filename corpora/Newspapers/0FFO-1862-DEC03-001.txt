BENEVOLENT or STRANGERS' FRIEND
SOCIETY.—The distress in Lancashire has induced this Society
to defer its advertisements till necessity compels it to APPEAL to the
public in behalf of the distress in London. Above 2.700 cases of ex¬
treme want during the summer months have so exhausted its funds.
that, to meet an average expenditure of £300 during December and
January, it had, at the date of the Report (Nov. 17), only £11 15s.
Immediate assistance is urgently required to meet the sad cases of
severe privation which winter is sure to bring. Contributions of either
money or clothing (new or old) will be thankfully received at the office
of the Society, 7, Exeter-hall, W. C.
W. J. CHAMPION, Secretary.
