PORT of LONDON LOCAL MARINE BOARD.—
Election of Members.—The Lord Mayor herebt
ao ines notice, that
the TRIENNIAL ELECTION of SIX MEMRERS to the LOCAL
MARINE BOARD of the PORT of LONDON, pursuant to the provi¬
sions of "The Merchant Shipping Act. 1854," will take place at the
Mansion-house, on Monday, the 25th day of January instant, at 11
o'clock in the forenoon, where and when all persons whose names ap¬
near on the Revised List of Voters now in force may record their vote
or votes for any one or more persons, not exceeding six, to be Members
of such Board; every male who is by such list entitled to vote being
qualified to be elected a member.
The mode of conducting the election will be as follows:—
1. The votes will be taken by means of voting papers, signed by the
voters, a printed form of which, with a copy of the list of voters, may
be obtained on application at this office one week before the day of
election.
.The voter must personally deliver his voting paper, properly filled
un and signed, to the undersigned (who has been appointed to receive
he votes) at the Mansion-house.
3. The election will finally close at 3 o'clock in the afternoon, after
which hour no voting paper will be received.
By order,
GEORGE C. OKE, Chief Clerk to the Lord Mayor.
Justice-room, Mansion-house, 13th January, 1869.
