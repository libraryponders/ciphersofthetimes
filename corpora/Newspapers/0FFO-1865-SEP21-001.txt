OLD HUMMUMS HOTEL, Covent-garden, Lon¬
don, 15th September, 1865.—Notice to Customers.—The pro¬
brietor begs to return sincere thanks for the kind support enjoyed for
so many years, and regrets to announce that the HOTEL will be
CLOSED on Monday, the 18th instant, in consequence of the expira¬
tion of the lease, of which a renewal cannot be obtained as the house is
about to be pulled down the site being required by the Duke of Bed¬
ford for the extension of Covent-garden-market. All articles of luggage
not removed from the hotel by the 29th instant will be deposited at a
warehouse, and if not claimed within 12 months will be sold to defray
expenses. It is requested that all outstanding accounts may be paid
to Messrs. Few and Co., solicitors, 2, Henrietta-street, Covent-garden,
W.C., to whose care all communications may be addressed.
