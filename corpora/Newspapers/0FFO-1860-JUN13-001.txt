NOTICE.—Caution against Forgery and Frand.—

cute parties, in order to put a stop to the nefarious practice of selling,
as his BAKING POWDER, spurious proffuctions, in imitation packets

Sessions, March 5th last, and sentenced to three years' penal servitude.
