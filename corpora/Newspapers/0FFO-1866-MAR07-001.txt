THE SURGICAL AID SOCIETY, 16, Ludgate¬
hill (corner of Ave Maria-lane).
President—The Right Hon. the Earl of SHAFTESBURY, K.G.
Mechanical supports are provided by the Society to the poor, who,
from the constant misery of pain, being unable to work, either become
a heavy burden on their relations or invalid inmates of the poor-house.
With the exception of societies whose sole object is the supply of
trusses, this is the only institution in England framed to meet this
great want.
The public hospitals send patients to this Society, as their functions
do not extend to this class of relief.
Subscriptions and donations are earnestly solicited, and will be
thankfully received by the Treasurer, W. Gray, Esq., 2, St. Peter's-alley.
Cornhill; by the bankers, Messrs. Barclay and Co., Lombard-street; or
by the Secretary, at the offices of the Society.
