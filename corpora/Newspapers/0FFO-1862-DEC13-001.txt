ANILINE DYES.—GIRARD's PATENT for IM¬
PROVEMENTS in PREPARING COLOURING MATTERS
for DYEING and PRINTING.—The undersigned acknowledge having
INFRINGED this PATENT, by making and selling dyes manufac¬
tured according to Girard's process; and, in consideration of Messrs.
Simpson, Maule, and Nicholson withdrawing proceedings taken by
them against me for so doing, I hereby express my regret for having
thus invaded their rights, as sole vendors of these dyes, and declare
that I have discontinued to make or sell such dyes, and will not do so
during the term of the above mentioned patent. —Dated this 28th day
of November, 1862.
EDW. KEIGHLEY.
