ST. LUKE's, Old-street, Middlesex.—The Church¬
wardens of the said parish, having been ordered by Her Majesty
n Council to adopt measures for CLOSING UP the VAULTS under
St. Luke's Church, situate in Old-street, in the county of Middlesex,
and that the coffins contained in the same vaults should be covered
with fresh earth and powdered charcoal, are about to carry the said
order into immediate effect, and hereby give notice that the relatives or
friends of deceased persons, whose remains may be deposited in the
said vaults, desiring to remove the same to any other burial place, will
be permitted to do so under proper and legal regulations, on making ap,
plication to the Churchwardens for that purpose, on or before the 1st
day of March next, after which time no remoyal from the said vaults
will be allowed. — Dated the 31st day of January, 1860.
JOHN PARSON, Vestry Cle.
