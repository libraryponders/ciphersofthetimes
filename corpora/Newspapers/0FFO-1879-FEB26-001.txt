COUNTY of MIDDLESEX to wit.—The Contagious
Diseases (Antmals) Act. 1878.—The DAIRIES, COWSHEDS,
and MILK SHOPS ORDER of 1879.—At a Meeting of the Executive
Committes of the Local Authority for the County, held at the Ses¬
sions-house. Clerkenwell, on Monday, the 24th day of February, 1873.
it was resolved and ordere as follow
That the following RULES and REGULATIONS shall take effect
and be in force on and after the 1st day of March, 1879, with regard to
dairies and cow sheds in the occupation of persons following the
trade of cowkeepers or dairymen, and with regard to m ik stores, milk
shops, and vessels, used for containing milk for sale by such persons.
1. All daines and cowsheds in the occupation of persons following
the trade of cowkeepers or dairymen and all milk stores and milk shops
shall be duly and properly cleansed at leist once every day to the sati¬¬
faction of the District Inspector of the Local Authority.
. 2. All vessels used for containing milk for sale py such persons shall
be thoroughly cleansed by the application of bolling water or steam.
By order of the Executive Committee.
NICHOLSON, Clerk of the Peace.
Sestions-house, Clerkenwell, 24th February, 1879.
