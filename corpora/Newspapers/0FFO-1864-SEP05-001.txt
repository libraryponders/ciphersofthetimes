cation having been made to the Governors of the Bank of Eng¬
land to direct the re-transfer from the Commissioners for the Reduction
of the National Debt of the sum of £308 16s. 10d. Consolidated Three
per Cent. Annuities, heretofore standing in the name of Col. JOHN
OCTAVIUS GLOVER, of Cambridge, deceased, and which was trans¬
ferred to the said Commissioners, in consequence of the dividends

paid, to the Rev. John Glover, the acting executor, who has claimed the
same, unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and make out
his claim thereto.
