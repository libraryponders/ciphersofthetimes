CHARITY COMMISSION.—In the Matter of the

blishment of Scholarships at University College, London, called The
ATKINSON MORLEY SURGICAL SCHOLARSHIPS.—By direc¬
tion of the Board of Charity Commissioners for England and Wales,
notice is hereby given that an Order is Proposed to be made by them,
after the expiration of one calendar month, to be computed from the
first publication of this notice, ESTABLISHING a SCHEME for the
REGULATION of the abovementioned Charity.
A Draft of the proposed Scheme may be inspected free of cost, daily,
except on Sundays, during the space of 21 days from the first publica¬
tion of this notice, at the office of Messrs. Cookson, Wainewright, and
Pennington, 6, New-square, Lincoln's-inn, W.C. ; and at the office of
the Charity Commissioners, No. 8, York-street, St. James's-square,
between the hours of 10 a.m. and 4 p.m. respectively.
Any objections to the proposed Order, or suggestions for its modifi¬
cation, may be transmitted, in writing, to the Secretary of the said
Commissioners, No. 8, York-street, St. James's-square, London, within
21 days next after the first publication of this notice.
