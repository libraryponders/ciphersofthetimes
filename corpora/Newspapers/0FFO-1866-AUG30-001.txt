RIGADUNABURG RAILWAY COMPANY.—
The firm of S. H. Heimann and Co., in Wilna, having duly made
known to the Direction of the Rigs-Diinaburg Railway Company, by
the delivery of the proper interest, coupons, and scrip of the shares
Nos. 73,855 to 73,871, that the above 17 shares have been lost in trans¬
mission, and petitioned that other shares be issued in lien thereof,
the Direction, in conformity with Art No. 23 of the statutes of the
Company, hereby publicly notify the same, and the public is hereby in¬
formed that all objections to such re-issue, founded upon whatsoever
plea, must be sent in within the period of 12 months from the date of
the third and last publication of this advertisement; after that period
no further re claim can be entertained, and should the above men¬
tioned shares, sub Nos. 73,855 to 73,871, and which (according to the re¬
gister of shares) were issued to Mr. Pogarelsky, College Counsellor, still
exist, the same shall be held as cancelled and void, and new shares
will be issued in their stoad to the firm of S. H. Heimann and Co., in
conformity with the statutes of the Company.
Riga, the 10th August, 1866.
