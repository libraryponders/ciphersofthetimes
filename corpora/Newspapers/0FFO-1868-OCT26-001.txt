BILLIARDS.—Roberts, Champion of the World,
and W. Duiton will give a SCIENTIFIC DISPLAY, at the
Assembly Rooms, Eyre Arms, St. John's-wood, on Thursday next,
October 29. Programme:—Morning, commence 2 p.m., Billards,
500 up, the Champion giving 150 points; and 11 games of Pyramids,
Dufton receiving one ball each game. On which occasion ladies will
be admitted and smoking prohibited. Evening—A Match of 1,000 up.
the Champion giving 300 points. The winner will be presented with a
handsome prize by the proprietor. After which five games of Pyramids
will be played as a finale. Commence at 7.30. An interval will take
place between each entertainment, during which time the Champion
will display his marvellous feats with the hand. Admission, 5s. ; re¬
served seats, 7s. 6d. (morning or evening), or double tickets for both,
7s. 6d. and 10s. Reserved seats obtainable at the Eyre Arms only,
where a plan may be seen and seats secured. N.B. All applications
by post will be promptly attended to. A magnificent billard table will
be erected expressly for the occasion by Burroughes and Watts, also
their new patent marking board.
