RESOLUTION at a PUBLIC MEETING of SUB¬
SCRIBERS to the CUBBON MEMORIAL, held at Bangalore,
on Saturday, the 16th August, 1862, for the purpose of deciding on the
fittest manner of perpetuating the memory of the late Sir Mark
Cubbon, K.C.B., Lieutenant-General and Commissioner of Mysore
and Coorg.
That this Meeting, in recognition of the noble character, the emi¬
nent abilities, and great public services of the
te S ir Mark Cubbon,
K.C.B., Commissioner of Mysore, is desirous of perpetuating his
memory in the most lasting manner and therefore resolves that a
statue, by one of the first sculptors of the age, be erected in Bangalore.
in front of the new offices of the Government, from the funds placed at
the disposal of the Joint-Committee of the Memorial."
Subscriptions for the statue will be received by Messrs. Arbuthnot,
Latham, and Co., 33, Great St. Helen's, London, E.C.; or Major C.P.
Taylor, Secretary to the Memorial, Bangalore.
Bangalore, August 24, 1862.
