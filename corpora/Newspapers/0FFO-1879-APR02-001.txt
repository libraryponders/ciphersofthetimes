CITY of LONDON and LIBERTIES.—The Dairies,
Cowsheds, and Milk Shops Order of 1879.—In pursuance of the pro¬
visions of this order, made by Her Majesty's Privy Council on the 6th
February, 1879, the Markets Committee of the Corporation of the
City of London hereby give notice, that they will, on and after the
1st of April, 1879, open and thenceforth keep a REGISTER for the
registration of persons carrying on, within the City of London and the
Liberties thereof, the trades of COWKEEPERS, DAIRYMEN, or
PURVEYORS of MILK, and that they will be prepared, during the
month of April next ensuing, to register such persons, on application
being made to the Committee upon the form provided for the
purpose.
If any person or persons carry on within the City of London and the
Liberties thereof, at any time after the 30th April next, any of the
trades aforesaid, without being duly registered, he or they will be
liable to a penaity not exceeding £20.
