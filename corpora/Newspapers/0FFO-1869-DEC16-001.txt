dends were paid over to the Commissioners for the Reduction of the

claimed since the 5th April, 1859; Notice is hereby given, that, on the

paid to Josiah Wilson, the surviving executor of George Carter, de¬
ceased, who has claimed the same, unless some other claimant shall
sooner appear and make out his claim thereto.
