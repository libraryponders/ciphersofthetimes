—Many persons in the country ask, "What is the private inquiry

no form or shape connected with the police. It has, however, un¬
ravelled the Smyth forgeries, the Worcester forgeries the Rugeley
murders, incendiary fires, divorce and libel cases.—C. NICHOLS.
