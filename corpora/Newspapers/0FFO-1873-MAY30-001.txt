THE CORPORATION of LLOYD'S hereby give

That, on and after Tuesday, the 3d June, 1873, a stamp bearing the
words "Subscribed by Underwriting Members of Lioyd's only, 34 and
35 Vic., cap. 21, sec. 31," will be used by the Members of the Corpora¬
tion to distinguish marine insurance policies underwritten by them.
That, under the provisious of Lloyd's Act of Incorporation, above¬
mentioned, any person, without the authority of the Society, imitating
or copying or offering or uttering or using any form of policy bearing
this stamp is liable, for every such offence, on summary conviction
before two justices, to a penalty not exceeding twenty pounds.
That, on and after Tuesday, the 3d June, 1873, an office will be
open at Lioyd's, where all blank policy forms intended for the use of
Underwriting Members of Lioyd's can be stamped free of charee.
By order, B. CHARLES STEPHENSON,
