DR. PIRSCHER, at present of 7, Cardington-street,
Hampstead-road, begs leave to inform his acquaintances that
there is no Pirscher in this country but himself. His relatives, the
officers of his name in the Prussian and Brunswick services, have
never resided in England. Dr. P. has no intercourse with, nor are
his affairs known to, any German in England. He is an old man, and
has lived above 25 years in England.
