calling himself Ludlow). He was last seen at Barnsbury-park, Isling¬
ton, on Sunday, the 15th March last, at about a quarter before 9
o'clock in the morning when he hired a Hansom cab. The said
Frederick Grogg is an Irishman, about 23 or 24 years of age, about 5
feet 6 or 7 inches high, low forehead, dark hair and parted in the
