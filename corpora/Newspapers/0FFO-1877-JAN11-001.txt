THE HOLBORN UNION, in the county of Middle¬
sex.—Infirmary at Highgate.—Whereas, the Guardians of the
foor of the said Holborn Union are desirous to ENLARGE their
WORKHOUSE or HOSPITAL, situate at or near Highgate, in the
parish of St. Mary Islington, in the county of Middlesex, and for
that purpose toACQUIRE the LAND and HEREDITAMENTS here¬
inafter described. Notice is hereby given, that the said Guardians
propose to acquire and take under the provisions of the Metropo¬
litan Poor Act, 1867, the Poor Law Amendment Act, 1868, and of
the Lands Clanses Consolidation Act, 1845, and the Lands Clauses
Consolidation Act Amendment Act, 1860, all that piece of land, com¬
prising 1 acre and 20 perches, or thereabouts, situate at or near High¬
gate, and in the parish of St. Mary, Islington, in the county of Midale¬
sex, abutting on or towards the north-east on the Archway-road, on
or towards the north-west on a brickfield, on or towards the south¬
west on the grounds of College-house, the property of the said Guar¬
dians, and on or towards the south-east on a strip of land abutting on
Archway-road aforesaid. The object for which the said piece of and
is proposed to be taken is for the enlargement of the Workhouse or
Hospital of the said Guardians standing on lands contiguous thereto,
and known as College-house. The quantity of land required is 1 acre
and 20 perches or thereabouts. A plan of the saidlland proposed to
be acquired or taken is open for inspection daily at No. 23, Ely-place,
in the county of Middlesex, E.C., between the hours of 10 a.m. and
4 p.m.—Dated this 1st day of January, 1877.
JAMES, CURTIS, and JAMES, Solicitors to the
saidGuardians.
