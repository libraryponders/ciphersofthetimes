TRALIA.—The ACT to Amend the "Northern Territory Act,"
passed by the Parliament of South Australia, and assented to by
His Excellency the Acting Governor on the 24th day of November,
1868, is published by order of the Government of South Australia,
in The Times of 27th January, 1869, for the INFORMATION of all
persons being HOLDERS of LAND ORDERS in the Northern Ter¬
ritory of South Australia, whose special attention is requested to
Clause 5 of said Act. A copy of the Act has been forwarded to each

undersigned. Those land order holders who may not receive one,
consequent on their present address not being known, will have the
goodness to send their address to the undersigned, when they will be
immediately supplied with a copy of the Act.

of South Australia.
