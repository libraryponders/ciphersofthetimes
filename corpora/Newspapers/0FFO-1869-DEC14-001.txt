ADDITION to SURNAME.—Notice is hereby
given, that the Reverend HENRY MAXWELL EGAN, Master
of Arts and Clerk in Holy Orders, only son of the late W. M. Egan,
of Stillorgan, county of Dublin, Esq., last male descendant, and repre¬
sentative of his futher's family formerly curate in Newcastle-on-Tyne,
and of St. George's parish, Hanover-square, Middlesex, and now re¬
siding at Bencavin, county of Dublin, hath, for sundry family reasons,
and in compliance with the will and directions of his mother,
LASSUMED for himself and his heirs the SURNAME of DESMOND.
in addition to and after his paternal surname of Egan, anciently caled
Adeghan, and that he, the sail Henry Maxwell Egan—according to law
by deed poll under his hand and seal, bearing date the 1st day of
November, 1863, and registered in Her Majesty's High Court of Chan¬
cery, did solemnly declare that from the day of the date thereof, and
for and on behalf of himself, his wife, and lawful heirs, he assumed
said family name of Desmond, and did determine from time to time,
and at all times thereatter, and in all acts, writings, suits, claims, and
demands, and in respect of all rights and liabilities, and upon all occa¬
sions whassover, to use and subscribe, and to be called, known, and
distinguished by said surname of Desmond after and in addition to the
surnaine of Egan; so that in all things as aforesaid he may assert his
rights, and be responsible for all just and legal claims against him, as
Henry Maxwell Egan Desmond, instead of, as heretofore, Henry W.
Maxwell Egan.—Signed and sealed this 1st day of November, 1867.
HENRY MAXWELL EGAN DESMOND.
