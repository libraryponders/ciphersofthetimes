hereby given, that the Trustees of the abovementioned Charities pro¬

LEASE of so much of the PROPERTY mentioned in the subjoined
Schedule as belongs to the said Charities, the residue thereof belonging
to the Drapers' Company in their own right, for the term of 80 years
from the 29th day of September, 1871, at the annual rent of a pepper¬
corn for the first year of the said term, £1,000 for the second year of

said property, unless some sufficient objection to the grant of such
lease shall be made known to the said Commissioners, within 21 days

friars, E.C.

Secretary, No. 8, York-street, St. James's-square, London.
