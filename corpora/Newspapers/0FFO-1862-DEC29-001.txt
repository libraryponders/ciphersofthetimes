GATE CONWAY, Liverpool.—The next SESSION of this In¬
stitution will COMMENCE on the 31st January, 1863.
The Conway, moored in the river Mersey, is designed to train and
educate at the most moderate cost, boys intended for officers in the
merchant navy.
The course of two years in the Conway is reckoned by a special order
of the Board of Trade as one year at sea. Thus the pupils save a year
in passing their examinations to be officers, and require to be at sea
only three instead of four years before doing so.
