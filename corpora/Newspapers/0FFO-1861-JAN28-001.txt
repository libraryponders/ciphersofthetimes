TO Mr. JOSEPH TUCKFIELD, of 6, Goswell-road,
Watch Manufacturer. —I hereby RETRACT all STATEMENTS
that I have made PREJUDICIAL to your POSITION as a MANU¬
FACTURER. From information I have recently received I am
