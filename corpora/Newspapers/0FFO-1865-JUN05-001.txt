Channel, leaving the Gunfleet Light-house and all the buoys of the

hand, into Harwich Harbour, to finish between the Guard Ship and
the Commodore's Yacht, the Violet, in two classes, viz:—
First-class, schooners and yawls. Time for tonnage, quarter
minute per ton up to 150 tor
