THE CANCER HOSPITAL.—It having become
known that some person or persons have been calling from house
to house in different neighbourhoods, asking for subscriptions for the
Cancer Hospital, the Committee deem it right to CAUTION the
public, and to state that no such applications are authorized by them.
The Cancer Hospital was founded in 1851, and is opposite the Con¬
sumption Hospital at Brompton. Subscriptions are only received by
the Treasurer, Bankers, or Secretary, at the office of the Hospital,
No. 167, Piccadilly, London W.
