MIDDLESEX.—To Wit.—Notice is hereby given,
that the GENERAL and QUARTER SESSIONS of the
PEACE holden for the County of Middlesex, for the purposes of
Criminal Business and Appeals, will, after the Adjourned May Session
(beginning on Tuesday, the 22d May instant), be held until further
notice at the Guildhall, Westminster. All committals after the 22d
instant should be made to Westminster, and all recognisances of ball
and recognisances to prosecute, to appeal, or to give evidence, should
after the same date be made returnable at Westminster. Depositions,
convictions, recognisances, and other documents required to be lodged
with the Clerk of the Peace should also be sent to Westminster. The
office of the Clerk of the Peace will be removed from the Sessions¬
house, Clerkenwell, to the Guildhall, Westminster, on the 1st June,
and any communications, letters, or inquiries intended for the Clerk
of the Peace should after the 1st June be addressed to Westminster,
The first Session to be held at Westminster will be the June General
Session, beginning on the 4th proximo. —Dated this 10th day of May,

 RICHD. NICHOLSON Clerk of the Peace.
