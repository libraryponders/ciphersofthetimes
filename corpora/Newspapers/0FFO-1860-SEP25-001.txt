BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividend.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of one Dividend on the sum of £2,636
16s. 1d. Consolidated 3 per Cent. Annuities, heretofore standing in the
name of ELIZABETH GILKES, of Brighton, Sussex, widow, deceased,

unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and make out his
