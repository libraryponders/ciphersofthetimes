GENTLEMAN LOST.—Mysteriously DISAP¬
PEARED, a GENTLEMAN, living in Bradford, Yorkshire,
His friends feel convinced that he has lest his life by accident, or met
with foul play. He was last seen on Saturday, 28th April, about 1 in
the afternoon, in Market-street, Bradford. Any INFORMATION
will be thankfully received and REWARDED. Address Chief Con¬
stable, at Bradford, Yorkshire, or at Leeds, Yorkshire. Description:
—Very short (about 5 feet or a little more), slight, fair complexion,
dark hair, thin beard, large forehead, dark eyes, near sighted, wore
steel spectacles. Had on light overcoat of a reddish brown, dark morn¬
ing coat and waistcoat, light trousers, ordinary black silk hat, small
gold watch, with gold Albert chain, having a key or pendant attached
by a steel ring. Quick, active manners, and intelligent expression.
