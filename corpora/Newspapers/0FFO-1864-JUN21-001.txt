CAUTION.—Dr. PIRSCHER, at present at
Merthyr, Glamorganshire, begs leave to inform his acquaintances
that there is no Pirscher in this country but himself. His relatives,
the officers of his name in the Prussian and Brunswick services, have
never resided in England. Dr. P. has no intercourse with, nor are his
affairs known to any German in England. He is an old man, and has
lived above 25 years in Great Britain.
