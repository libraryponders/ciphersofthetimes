WILLIAM OGDEN DAVIES.—This gentleman, a
son of Richard and Mary Davies, was born in Sackville-street,
London, on October 7, 1809. He married, about the year 1826, a Miss
Olivicrez. The only issue of the marriage was a daughter, whose
Christian name, it is believed, was Louisa, born within a year of the
marriage. It is not known where the marriage was solemnized, nor
where the child was born, nor has she been seen or heard of for nearly
50 years. William Ogden Davies died May 15, 1843. His DAUGHTER
above referred to, if living, or any person who can give information
concerning her is requested to COMMUNICATE with Messrs.
Capron, Dalton, Hitchins, and Brabant, of Savile-place, Conduit¬
street, London, W.. Solicitors.
