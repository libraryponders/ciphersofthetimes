Notice is hereby given, that an ELECTION of SIX MEMBERS
of the Local Marine Board of the Port of London will be held under
the provisions of "The Merchant Shipping Act, 1854," at the Guildhall,
in the city of London, on Monday, the 26th day of January inst.,
between the hours of 12 o'clock at noon and 3 o'clock in the afternoon.
Every person named in the Revised List of Voters is qualified to vote
at the election of members of the Local Marine Board, and is re¬
quested tobring a paper with the names of the persons for whom he votes
written thereon, and signed with his name and address. A cony of the
list will be delivered to any voter applying for the same at the Remem¬

TUTION during the past quarter:—Lifeboat at Whitby saved 12 men:
