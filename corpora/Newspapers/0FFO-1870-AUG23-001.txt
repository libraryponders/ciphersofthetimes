IN the HIGH COURT of ADMIRALTY of
ENGLAND.—No. 5.366.—The NORMANDY and the MARY.—
Whereas, in a cause of limitation of liability instituted in the High
Court of Admiralty of England on behalf of the London and South¬
Western Railway Company, the owners of the late steamship Nor¬
mandy, as plaintiffs, against Edward Jordan Hough, George Alexander
Laws, and Robert Watson Surtees, all of North Shields, in the county
of Northumberland, shipowners, the owners of the steamship Mary,
and against the owners of the cargo of the said steamship Mary, the
plaintiffs in a cause entitled the "Normandy," No. 5,359, and against
all and every other person or persons whatseever interested in the said
vessels Mary and Normandy, or having any right, claim, or interest
whatever with reference to or arising out of a collision which occurred
on or about the 17th day of March, 1870, between the said xeesels
Normandy and Mary, the Judge of the said Court did, on the 14th day
of July, 1870, apong other things, pronounce that in respect of loss of
life, or personal injury, or of loss or damage to ship's goods, mer¬
chandise, or other things, caused on the occasion of the collision afore¬
said, the owners of the said steam vessel Normandy, if answerable, are
only answerable in damages to an amount not exceeding six thousand
three hupdved and seventy-six pounds (£6.376), such sum being at the
rate of fifteen pounds (£15) for each ton of the registered tonnage of
the said steam vessel Normandy, without deduction on account of
engine room; and he ordered that the owners of the said steam vessel
Normandy do forthwith pay into Court-the said sum of six thousand
three hunded and seventy-six pounds


thereon at the rate of four per cent, per annum from the date of the
aforesaid collision until such payment into Court, and condemn the
plaintiffs in the costs of this suit.—This is therefore to give notice to
all persons having any CLAIM in respect of the LOSS or DAMAGE


the said cause on or before the 10th day of November, 1870, they will be
excluded from sharing in the aforesaid amount.—Dated this 15th day
of August. 1870.
H. C. ROTHERY, Registrar.
