CRYSTAL PALACE.—BLONDIN's extraordinary
PERFORMANCES on the rope 160 feet from the ground con¬
tinue to excite the liveliest interest. At every appearance he exhibits
some new and daring feats unattempted by any of his imitators. The
ascent on Wednesday will take place on the rope in the grounds
which has recently been considerably elevated. Tickets for reserved
seats at 1s., 2s. 6d., and 5s. each, are now ready. The distance traversed
by Blondin at each of these performances, on a single cord, is nearly
three quarters of a mile.
