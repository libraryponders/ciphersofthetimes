COMPTROLLER of REVENUES of the PROVINCE
of BUENOS AIRES. In accordance with Article 11 of the
Decree of the current year, regulating the Laws on Stamped Paper
the Comptroller of Revenues hereby gives notice, that for all BILLS
of EXCHANGE drawn in the Province of Buenos Aires in more
than one copy, the necessary STAMPS will be delivered for the
seconds and thirds of each set without any additional charge.
In virtue of the above the Comptroller also gives notice that all
Bills or Drafts presented without the proper stamp of the Province
applicable to the first, second, or third copies is in contravention of
the said Law, and in case of protest no legal preceedings can be taken
without first paying the penalty imposed in such cases in Article 21 of
the aforesaid Law, viz, ten times the amount of the stamps.
E. O'GORMAN, Comptroller of Revenues.
Buenos Aires, 2nd January, 1877.
