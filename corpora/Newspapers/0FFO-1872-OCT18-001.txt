BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividend.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of one dividend on the sum of £3,500
New £3 per Cent. Annuities, heretofore standing in the name of
JOHN BATH, of Farningham, Kent, farmer, and which dividend
was paid over to the Commissioners for the Reduction of the National
Debt in consequence of its having remained unclaimed since the 5th
April, 1862;—Notice is hereby given that, on the expiration of three
months from this date, the said Dividend will be paid to Maria Bath,
widow, administratrix of John Bath, deceased, who has claimed the
same, unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and make out
his claim thereto.

give notice that I have appointed the AUDIT of the ACCOUNTS of
