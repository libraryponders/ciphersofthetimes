BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividends.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of 10 dividends on the sum of
£297 15s. 4d. Consoldated Three per Cent. Annuities, heretofore stand¬
ing in the names of Rev. HENRY BELMONT SIMS of Great Parn¬
don Rectory, near Harlow, Essex, and FRANK HENRY JOHNSON,
of Hubbard's-hall, Harlow, Esq., and which dividends were paid over
to the Commissioners for the Reduction of the National Debt, in

three months from this date, the said Dividends will be paid to
Henry Frank Johnson, otherwise Frank Henry Johnson, the survivor,
who has claimed the same, unless some other claimant shall soonenap¬
pear and make out his claim thereto.
