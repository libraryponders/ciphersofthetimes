Ireland.—I beg to ACKNOWLEDGE, with thanks, the RE¬
CEIPT of £200 from the Baroness Burdett Coutts towards the Fund
for providing better boats and fishing gear for the distressed fishermen
of these islands, also £50 towards the Fund required to defray a

will be sent to any one wishing to learn further detaila concerning
