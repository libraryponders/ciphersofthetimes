COUNTY of MIDDLESEX to WIT.—Notice is
hereby given, under the provisions of the Prisons Apt, 1866 (28
and 29 Vict., cap. 126, s. 34), that a CHANGE has been MADE as to
the PRION to which prisoners committed within the jurisdiction of
the Justices in Quarter essions assembled for the County of Middle¬
sex (being the prison authority for the said county) may be sent by
reason of such autuority having appropriated a portion of one of the
prisons of the said authority—that is to say, the House of Detention,
Clerkenwell, in the said county, to a particular class of prisoners—that
is to say, to male prisoners convicted of felonies, misdemeanours, or
other crimes or offences, who shall have been in the first instance duly
committed to the House of Correction at Coldbath-fields, in the said
county, and who shall, after their committal to the last-mentioned
prison, be, from time to time, removed from the said House of Correc¬
tion to the said House of Detention during their terms of imprison¬
ment by virtue of orders for their removal to the said House of Deten¬
tion, duly made by the Just ces for the said county in Sessions
assembled, as provided by the said Act. Dated this 5th day of Fep¬
ruary, 1877.
RICHARD NICHOLSON, Clerk of the Peace.
