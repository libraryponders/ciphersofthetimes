Indo-European Family. 4. The Birth of the Mother Tongue. 5. The
Growth of the Mother Tongue. 6. The Kindred of the Mother
Tongue. 7. The Anglo-Saxon of the Peasantry. 8. Anglicisms—

Irish Accent. 11. Public Reading and Speaking. 12. Composition—

gentlemen are admitted. The place will probably be Willis's rooms,

to the lecturer, at 29, Montague-street, on Saturday, 7th Nov., at 1.
