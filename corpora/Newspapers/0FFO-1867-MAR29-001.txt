the FISHERY DISTRICT of the RIVERS KENT, Bela,
Winster, Leven, and Duddon shall be as follows:—So much of the
rivers Kent, Bela, Winster, Leven, and Duddon and their tributaries
and lakes as is situate within the counties of Westmoreland, Lancaster,
and Cumberland, and also the estuaries of the said rivers, and so much
of the coast as lies between the northern boundary of the Lune, Wyre,
Keer, and Cocker fishery district in Morecambe Bay and Haverigg
Point, at the northern boundary of the estuary of the said river Dud¬
sion, in the county of Cumberland, and all streams flowing into the sea,
between the said points and being within the said counties as laid
down on the map deposited in the office of the Clerk of the Peace for
the county of Westmoreland.
