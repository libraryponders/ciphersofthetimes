BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividends.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of eight dividends on the sum of £20
Long Annuities, heretofore standing in the name of WILLIAM
SEAMAN, of Sutton-street, Clerkenwell, gentleman, deceased, and
which dividends were paid over to the Commissioners for the Reduc¬
tion of the National Debt in consequence of the first thereof having
remained unclaimed since the 10th October, 1856;—Notice is hereby
given, that, on the expiration of three months from this date, the said
Dividends will be paid to Thomas Charles Blundell and Henry Thomas
Berry, the executors, who have claimed the same, unless some other
claimant shall sooner appear and make out his claim thereto.

the NEXT of KIN (if any) and all others having an interest in
the personal estate and effects of ROBERT WOLFE, late a passenger
on board the ship Sussex, who died at sea, on the 8th February, 1864.
