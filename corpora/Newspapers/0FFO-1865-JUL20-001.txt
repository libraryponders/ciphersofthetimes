MAMBETH OLD BURIAL GROUND, High¬
street, Lambsth.—The Burial Board for the parish of St. Mary,
Lambeth, hereby give notice, that it is their intention to put the above
burial ground in decent order, under the powers vested in them by the
18th and 19th Vic., cap 128, section 18, and all persons who are the
holders of private graves, vauits, tombs, or head and foot stones in the
said ground, and are desirous of reinstating the same, are required to
communicate with the Clerk to the said Board, at the office of the
Lambeth Burial Board, at the Vestry-hall, Kennington-green, Lam¬
beth, S., by the 24th of July, 1865, otherwise the said Board will not be
responsible for any damage done to any private grave, vault, tomb, or
head and foot stone.
By order of the Board,		ALFRED E. T. WORLEY, Clerk.
Vestry-hall, Kennington-green, S., July 5, 1865.
