A COURT of MAYOR and ALDERMEN, held in
the Inner Chamber of the Guildhall, of the city of London, on
Wednesday, the 11th day of December, 1861, and in the 25th year of
the Reign of Victoria, of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and
Ireland, Queen, &c.,
Resolved unanimously,—
That this Court, in recording the lamented death of Sir Peter
Laurie, Knight, Alderman, and President of Bridewell and Bethlem
Hospitals, desire to express their deep sense of the valuable services of
their late brother during the long period he was a member of the
Court, and of the excellent manner in which he performed the many
and important duties which devolved upon him.
That this Court is anxious to convey to the family of the late
Alderman their sincere condolence on the loss of one so universally
esteemed and respected—so honourable and upright on all occasions.
