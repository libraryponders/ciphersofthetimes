NORTH-EAST LONDON RIFLES.—Special
Order. The Lieut.-Colonel commanding sincerely congratulates
the Regiment on the fact of the strength of the corps having been
increased by the War-office to 11 companies. He has also great satis¬
faction in announcing, that there is now only needed an addition of
about 86 recruits to entitle the corps to ask for a 12th company and a
second Lieut.-Colonel, an addition which, judging from the last year,
may be confidently looked forward to before the month of May next.
The Regiment also will be pleased to hear that the Hon. Colonel, who
has taken so lively an interest in the wellbeing of the corps, has kindly
expressed his gratification at the prospects and position of the Regi¬
ment, which, by the nominal roll in December last, numbered no less
than 623 efficients.
L
C. M. WALMISLEY, Captain and Adjutant,
Head Quarters, Shaftesbury-street, Hoxton, N.
