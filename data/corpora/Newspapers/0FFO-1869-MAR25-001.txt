ROYAL LONDON MILITIA.—Notice is hereby
given, that the MILITIA of the CITY of LONDON is appointed
to be TRAINED and EXERCISED (pursuant to the several Acts of
Parliament made in that behalf), for 27 days together, to commence
on Monday the 26th day of April, 1869, in addition to 14 days' preli¬
minary drill for recruits; and all men belonging to the said Militia are
hereby required to assemble at the Head-quarters, Artillery-ground,
City-road, in the county of Middlesex, at 10 o'clock on the morning of
the same day; and all recruits who have been enrolled since the com¬
mencement of the training of last year are required to assemble at the
Head-quarters aforesaid on Monday, the 17th day of April, at 10
o'clock in the morning, for the purpose of being drilled prior to the
assembly of the regiment on the 26th day of April aforesaid, and every
man, not labouring under any infirmity incapacitating him, who shall
not appear at the times and places aforesaid, will be deemed a deserter
and incur the penalties provided by law in such a case.
By order of Her Majesty's Lieutenants for the City of London,
HENLEY GROSE SMITH, Clerk.
