England to direct the payment of 19 Dividends on the Sum of £15 11s. 6d.
Annuity for a term of years now expired, heretofore standing in the
name of ANN DOROTHY KEELING, of Clifton, Glocestershire,

of the first thereof having remained unclaimed since the 10th of

unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and make out his
claim thereto.
