South Wales, widow and relict of Robert Campbell, late of the same
place, Esquire, deceased, made her last will at Sydney aforesaid, dated the
28th December, 1858, and left the said colony for England in the be¬
ginuing of the year 1859, and afterwards arrived there and resided for

her executors, or giving satisfactory information relative thereto, such
a sum as will fully and liberally reimburse them for all their expenses

Wilde, Rees, Humphry, and Wilde, 21, College-hill, London, solicitors
for the executors.
