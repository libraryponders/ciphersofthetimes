NOTICE to MAKERS and VENDORS of HARD
RUBBER or VULCANITE and HARD RUBBER GOODS.—
Whereas the SCOTTISH VULCANITE COMPANY, Limited, car¬
rying on business at Viewforth, near Edinburgh, are, in right of 17
letters patent of various dates, covering the manufacture of vulcanite,
and also its application for the manufacture of combs, telegraphic insu¬
lators, pistol handles, brushes and brush handles, and a variety of other
articles; and whereas the said, Company have been informed that
various persons in the United Kingdom have been infringing the said
patent rights, by vending and manufacturing and vending hard rubber
or vulcanite and hard rubber goods, such as combs, buttons, door
knobs, knife handles, &c.; notice is hereby given, that all persons so
infringing have subjected, and are subjecting, themselves in damages
to the said Company for their said illegal acts, and that the said
Vulcauite Company, limited, will proceed to recover said damages in
competent form; and further, that unless the said persons who have
committed a breach of the said Company's patent rights immediately
desist and cease from manufacturing the said hard rubber goods,
or vending such hard rubber or goods, other than the said Company's
manufacture, the Company will proceed at law against both makers
and vendors to have them restrained from such infringement.
May 5, 1863.
