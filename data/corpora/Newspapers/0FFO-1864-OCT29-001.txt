Reduction of the National Debt of the sum of £21 19s. 10d. New Three
per Cent. Annuities heretofore standing in the name of FREDE¬
RICK WILLIAM IRBY, of Boyland-hall, Norfolk, Esq., and which
was transferred to the said Commissioners, in consequence of the
dividends thereon having remained unclaimed since the 10th October,
1854;—Notice is hereby given, that, on the expiration of three months
from this date, the said Stock will be Transferred, and the Dividends
thereon paid, to the said Frederick William Irby, who has claimed
the same, unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and make
out his claim thereto.

—In consequence of the death of Mrs. Mary Ann Johnstone, the
abovenamed Charles John Cosen Johnstone (who left England in the
year 1844, and is believed to have gone to America), is required to

on her death; and take notice, that if the said Charles John Cosens

day of December, 1864, the executors named in the will of the said
Mary Ann Johnstone will tender her said will for probate, and proceed
to dispose of her property as if the said Charles John Cosens Johnstone
had died in her lifetime. Any person giving such information as will
lead to the proof of the death of the said Charles John Cosens John¬
stone (if dead) will be rewarded. Apply to Messrs. Tompson, Picker¬
ing, and Styan, 4, Stone-buildings, Lincoln's-inn, London.
