NORTH-EAST METROPOLITAN AUDIT
DISTRICT.-PARISH of ST.MATTHEW, BETHNAL-GREEN.
I the undersigned Thomas Wrake Ratcliff, attorney-at-law, auditor of
the abovenamed district, do hereby give notice that I have appointed
the AUDIT of the ACCOUNTS of the abovenamed parish, for the
half-year ended the 25th March, 1870, to commence on Friday, the 13th day
of May, 1870, at 12 o'clock noon, at the Workhouse of the said parish.
when and where all persons who by law are bound to account at such
audit are required to attend, and to submit all books, documents, bills,
and vouchers containing or relating to the accounts or to moneys
assessed for and applicable to, the relief of the poor. —Dated the 28th
day of April, 1870.		THOS. W. RATCLIFF, District Auditor.
