WISSING, since Sunday night, Nov. 27, from a pri¬
vate lunatic asylum, in the neighbourhood of London, a YOUNG
MARRIED LADY, of weak intellect, suffering under the delusion
that her child is somewhere hidden in the metropolis. This unfortu¬
nate lady is 23 years of age, has brown hair, large gray eyes, pale com¬
plexion, with a haggard expression of countenance, and stammers
when speaking. She has contrived, in order to facilitate her escape, to
change her clothes for those of one of the servants of the establish¬
ment, consisting of a dark cotton dress, striped woollen shawl, and
straw bonnet. A REWARD of FIFTY POUNDS will be given for
correct information as to her whereabouts, by applying to Mr. Pollaky,
private inquiry office, 13, Paddington-green, W. —Dec. 1, 1864.
