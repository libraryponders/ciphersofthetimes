LONDON and NORTH-WESTERN RAILWAY.—
Messrs. CHAPLIN and HORNE, as Agents to the London and
orth-Western Railway Company, and on their own behalf, respect¬
fully inform the public, that on and after 1st February, 1864, they will
not collect any goods or merchandise after 7 p.m.; also that they will
cease to receive at their offices and premises at 7.30 p.m.
The despatch of goods for other railways will continue as at present,
