MIDDLESEX.—Notice is hereby given, that a
REPORT, made under the statute, 4th Geo. IV., cap. 64, by the
Justices appointed at the last January Quarter Session of the Peace,
held in and for the county of Middlesex, to visit the HOUSE of COR¬
RECTION for the said county at Coldbath-fields, that the said House
of Correction is insufficient, inconvenient, and otherwise inadequate to
give effect to the rules and regulations prescribed by the above men¬
tioned statute, and the other acts relating to prisons, and that the same
requires to be enlarged, was laid before the justices for the said county
at the General Quarter Session of the Peace, holden in and for the
said county, by adjournment, on Thursday, the 17th day of April inst.,
and that it is the intention of the justices to take such report into con¬
sideration at the next General Session to be held in and for the said
county, by adjournment, on Thursday, the 22d day of May next, at the
Sessions-house, on Clerkenwell-green, at 12 o'clock at noon. —Dated

C. HEATON ELLIS, Clerk of the Peace for Middlesex.
Sessions-house, Clerkenwell.
