WOUNG's PARAFFINE OIL.—CAUTION.—In
Chancery: "Young and other v. Fernie and others."—His
Honour the Vice-Chancellor Stuart, having found a verdict in favour of
the plaintiffs in the above suit, on all the issues, and made a decree for
a perpetual injunction to restrain the defendants from infringing Mr.
James Young's Patent for England, and directing them to pay to the

EXHIBITION of VERMIN TRAPS.—The
ROYAL SOCIETY for the PREVENTION of CRUELTY to
ANIMALS have great pleasure in announcing that they have obtained
permission to EXHIBIT at the Royal Horticultural Gardens during

main entrance of the Horticultural Gardens, Exhibition-road.
