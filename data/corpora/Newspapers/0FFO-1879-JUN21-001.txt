Clare, on the fact that England's Navy is based solely on the entirety
of his patents, inventions, designs, models, &c., APPEALS to the
British Nation for a National Fund, commensurate with the benofits
that have arisen therefrom and the advantages henceforward accruing
thereon to Great Britain's national defences affoat in upholding the
peace of the world.—John Clare. H.M.S.B., Liverpool. N.B.—House

Clare than the majority of the House supposed.—Morning Papers.
