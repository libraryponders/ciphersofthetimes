TO SADDLERS, Harnessmakers, Bridle Cutlers, &c.
—Notice.—At a large MEETING of EMPLOYEES in the above
trades, held in the large coffee room at Cartal's Crown Hotel, Rupert¬
street, Haymarket, on Tuesday evening last, the 17th instant, it was
unanimously resolved,—
That this Meeting, fully appreciating the liberality of Messrs. Gibson
and Co., of New Coventry-street, W., Messrs. Garden and Son, of Pic¬
cadilly, W., Mr. Alex. Gordon, of Piccadilly and Lisle-street, W., Mr.
Moslin, of Great Windmill-Street, W., and Mr. Dale, of Rupert-street,
Haymarket, W., saddlers, &c., in voluntarily expressing their determi¬
nation to close their respective establishments at 3 o'clock p.m. on
Saturdays, instead of at 7 o'clock, as heretofore, being deeply sensible
of the great advantage it will be to the employées in their service,
respectively beg to tender to those gentlemen their sincere and heart¬
felt thanks.
