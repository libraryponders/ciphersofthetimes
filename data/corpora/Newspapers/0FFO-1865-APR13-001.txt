GUM BERLAND and WESTMORELAND
WRESTLING SOCIETY.—AGRICULTURAL-HALL.—The
numerous patrons and supporters of the above Society are respectfully
informed that neither expense nor pains have been spared on the part
of the Committee to render the anniversary, on Good Friday, worthy

ments have been made to secure every comfort and convenience to
all classes of spectators; and that the sports may be got through in good
