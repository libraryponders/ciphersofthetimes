furnish prompt and reliable information of those residing in England
or abroad. Solicitors through this agency will be enabled to sorvo

incendiarism abroad. The Private Continental Inquiry Office, how¬
ever, will not undertake any cases which properly come within the pro¬
vince of the regular police authorities. Translations from the Ger¬

addressed J. P. Pollaky, 14, George-street, Mansion-house, E.C.
