county of Middlesex, at the Sessions-house, Clerkenwell green, in the
said county, for an ORDER for STOPPING UP a certain PUBLIC
ROAD, Lane, or Highway, called or commonly known by the name of
Honey-lane, in the parish of St. Mary Abbotts, Kensington, in the

Fulham-road and the Richmond-road, and terminating in such roads

two justices having viewed the same, with the plan of the old and pro¬

R. GREEN, Clerk to the Vestry of St. Mary Abbotts,
