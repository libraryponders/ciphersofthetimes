CAUTION.—To Perfumers, Hosiers, Chymists, and
others.—It having been ascertained that Eau de Cologne in bottles
with labels which are fraudulent imitations of the label, name, and
trade mark of "Johann Maria Farina, Gegenulber dem Julichs Platz"
(in Cologne), have been offered for sale by a foreigner, who personally
calls at the shops of different tradesmen, all perfumers, hosiers, chymists,
and others are hereby CAUTIONED AGAINST PURCHASING
EAU de COLOGNE with such imitation labels from such foreigner or
otherwise, and also against purchasing or selling, or offering or ex¬
posing for sale any Eau de Cologne in bottles having thereon such or
any other imitation labels, as by so doing they render themselves liable
to proceedings in Chancery for an injunction and for damages. And any
persons to whom any Eau de Cologne in bottles with such labels may be
offered for sale are requested to give immediate information thereof to
Johann Maria Farina, Gegenuber dem Julichs Platz, Cologne, at his
only agency in London, 7, Old Jewry, London, E.C. ; or to Messrs.
Vallance and Vallance, solicitors, 20, Essex-Street, Strand, London.
JOHANN MARIA FARINA, Gegenuber dem Julichs Platz,
February, 1861.
