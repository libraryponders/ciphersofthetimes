unto T. NUGENT KENNEY, of Correndoo, in the county of Galway,
and of Hermitage-park, in the county of Dublin, Esq., and unto Isabel
Augusta Bruce, his wife, only child and heiress of the late Sir John
Kingsmill, formerly colonel of the Battic-Axe Guard, by Elizabeth
Catherine, his wife, daughter and heiress of the late Sir John Kings¬
mill, of Sidmanton, in the county of Southampton, Bart., her Royal
license and authority, bearing date at St. James's, the 18th day of
January, 1866, that the said T. Nugent Kenney, and his wife, the
aforesaid Isabel Augusta Bruce, may TAKE and henceforth USE the
SURNAME of KINGSMILL only, instead of that of Kenney, and
may also bear the arms of Kingsmill and Kenney quarterly, in ac¬
cordance with the desire of the aforesaid Elizabeth Catherine, Lady
Kingsmill, deceased. Provided that Her Majesty's concession and
declaration be recorded in the office of Ulster King of Arms in Ire¬
land, which has been done accordingly
J. BERNARD BURKE, Ulster.
