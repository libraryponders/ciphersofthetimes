CAUTION.—A quantity of spurious essences and
other articles of perfumery, intended for shipment and sale
abroad, upon which were affixed labels in imitation of the label and
trade mark and having the trade signature of Messrs. Piesse and
Lubin, of No. 2. New Bond-street, forged thereon, having been sent
into the port of London, the same has been seized and condemned by
Her Majesty's Commissioners of Customs for the port of London; and
as there is reason to believe that the practice of IMITATING the
TRADE MARE and FORGING the TRADE SIGNATURE of
Messrs. PIESSE and LUBIN is being carried on to a large extent:—
Notice is hereby given, that it is the intention of Messrs. Piesse and
Lubin to proceed, criminally or otherwise (as they may be advised),
against any person or persons who may hereafter sell, export for sale,
deal in, or otherwise dispose of any perfumery with any labels or
marks thereon or thereunto affixed having the name and address and
trade signature of their firm fraudulently and falsely printed, engraved,
or forged thereon. —Dated this 1st day of May, 1866.
VALLANCEnd VALLANCE, 20, Essex-Street, Strand,
London, Solicitors for Messrs. Piesse and Lubin.
