THIRTY POUNDS REWARD for the RECOVERY
of the BODY of a GENTLEMAN, supposed to have been drowned
off Selsey Bill, near Bognor, on Monday, the 16th of September. Had

lever watch with chain attached. The above reward will be given on
identification, or a liberal reward will be paid to any person giving
any genuine information that may lead to the recovery, by applying to
