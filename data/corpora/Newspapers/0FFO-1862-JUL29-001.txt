THE BALLOT.—A few friends of freedom of
election have formed themselves into a Committee for the pur¬
pose of presenting the Hon. F. H. F. BERKELEY, M.P. for the city
of Bristol, with a TESTIMONIAL for the courage zeal, and ability

party) during 25 years of Parliamentary life, through good report and
evil report. A list of the names of the Committee will shortly be pub¬
