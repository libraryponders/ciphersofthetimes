DISTRICT.—Edmonton Union.—I, the undersigned Thomas
Wrake Ratcliff, Attorney-at-law, auditor of the abovenamed district,
do hereby give notice that I have APPOINTED the AUDIT of the
ACCOUNTS of the EDMONTON UNION, and of the parishes therein
contained, for the half-year ended the 25th day of March, 1867, to com¬
mence on Thursday, the 16th day of May, 1867, at half-past 11 o'clock

day of May, 1867.
HOS, W. RATCLIFF, District Auditor.
