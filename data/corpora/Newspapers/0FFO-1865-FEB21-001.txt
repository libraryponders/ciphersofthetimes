PUBLIC APOLOGY.—I, the undersigned
ALFRED BARRETT, Senior of No. 51, Aldridge-road-villas,
Westbourne-park, in the parish of Paddington, in the county of
Middlesex, do hereby APOLOGIZE to Mr. Charles Keene, of No. 10,
Shrewsbury-villas, Talbot-road, Westbourne-grove, Bayswater, Lon¬
don, for the libels and slanders which I respectively, wrote, published,
and uttered against him on the 4th and 25th days of January last,
and every word of which I have since discovered, and do hereby
now acknowledge, to be totally and entirely devoid of truth; and
as the said Mr. Keene has agreed to forego proceeding with the
action which he has commenced against me, in the Court of Queen's
Bench, for the vindication of his character. I have paid him the sum
of £30, wherewith to pay the expenses he has been put to, his solici¬
tors charges, and the cost of having this apology published in such
newspapers as he may think proper. —Dated this 18th day of February,
in the year of our Lord 1863.
A. BARRETT.
