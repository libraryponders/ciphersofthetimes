£20 on application to Messrs. Yarborough and Wilkinson, 44, Lin¬
coln's-inn-fields. A further reward of £20 will be paid to any person
who will give such information as will enable Mr. Coleman to prosecute
to conviction the person or persons who organized and procured the as¬
semblage of the men for the above purposes.
