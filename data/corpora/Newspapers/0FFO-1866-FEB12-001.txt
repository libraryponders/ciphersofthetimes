BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividends.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank
of England to direct the payment of 14 Dividends on the sum of
£5 per annum Long Annuities, heretofore standing in the name of
THOMAS BLAKE, jun., late of Norwich, Esq., deceased, and which
dividends were paid over to the Commissioners for the Reduction of
the National Debt in consequence of the first thereof having re¬
mained unclaimed since the 10th October, 1853;—Notice is hereby
given that, on the expiration of three months from this date, the
said Dividends will be paid to Thomas Blake, one of the executors of
Thomas Blake, jun., deceased, who has claimed the same, unless some
other claimant shall sooner appear and make out his claim thereto.
