MIDDLESEX.—Notice is hereby given, that on
Thursday, the 15th day of July, 1869, at 12 at noon precisely, at
the Sessions House, at Clerkenwell, the Justices of the Peace for this
county in General Quarter Sessions assembled will take into their
consideration a certain PRESENTMENT, made on the 27th day of
May last, under the provisions of the Prisons Act, 1865, to the Jus¬
tices in General Sessions assembled, by two visiting justices of the
House of Correction for the said county of Middlesex, at Coldbath¬
fields, "That, in consequence of the insufficiency and inadequacy of
the said prison for the reception and maintenance therein of all the
male convicted prisoners of the said county, there is now a necessity
for the building of a new prison for the said county." —Dated this 12th
day of June, 1863.
RICHD. NICHOLSON, Clerk of the Peace.
