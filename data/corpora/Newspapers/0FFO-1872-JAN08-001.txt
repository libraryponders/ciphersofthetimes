MISSING.—WILLIAM FORBES, a middle-aged
man, about 55 years, a native of Thurso, Caithness, Scotland,
has ceased to correspond with his friends, who are most anxious to
know his present address. His last address was Forest-lane, Stratford,
London. His father is very weak; his mother is dead. If this comes to
his own hands, friends expect to hear from him immediately.—Thurso,
28th Dec., 1871.
