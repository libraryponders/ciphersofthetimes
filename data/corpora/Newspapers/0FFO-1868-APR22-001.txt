BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividend.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of one dividend on the sum of £219
Annuities for terms of years, heretofore standing in the name of
MARY ANN EVANS, of Aldgate, widow, and which dividend was
paid over to the Commissioners for the Reduction of the National
Debt in consequence of its having remained unclaimed since the 5th
April, 1855; Notice is hereby given, that on the expiration of three
months from this date, the said Dividend will be paid to Louisa
Harding Daiton, spinster, administratrix de bonis non of Mary Ann
Evans, widow, deceased, who has claimed the same, unless some other
claimant shall sooner appear and make out his claim thereto.
