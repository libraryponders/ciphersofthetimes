the undersigned Walter Spencer Hamilton, of The Firs, ledfont,
near Feltham, in the county of Middlesex, for having received into
my house and taken the care and charge of a Lunatic without having
first obtained an Order for reception and Medical Certificates, in com¬
pliance with the provisions of the Act of the 8th and 9th Victoria.
chapter 100, being an Act for the regulation of the care and treat¬
ment of Lunatics, as amended by the Act of the 16th and 17th
Victoria, chapter 96; and whereas in consideration of the Acknow¬
ledgment and Apology hereinafter set forth, the said Commissioners
have under the special circumstances of my case consented to stay
such proceedings:—Now I, the said Walter Spencer Hamilton, do
hereby ACKNOWLEDGE that I have VIOLATED the LAW by re¬
ceiving into my house and taking the care and charge of a Lunatic
without having first obtained the necessary Order and Medical Certi¬
ficates. And I do hereby express my deeptregret for having so done,
and do thus publicly Apologize for the same, and i do solemnly
promise not again to offend in like manner. —Dated this 5th day of

W. S. HAMILTON.
