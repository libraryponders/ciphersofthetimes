BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividends.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of 20 dividends on the sum of
£4 10s. Aunuity for term of years expired 10th October, 1859, hereto¬
fore standing in the name of ELIZABETH DAVIES, of Wanstead,
Essex, widow, and which dividends were paid over to the Commissioners
for the Reduction of the National Debt, in consequence of the first
thereof having remained unclaimed since the 5th April, 1850;—Notice
is hereby given that, on the expiration of three months from this
date, the said Dividends will be paid to the said Elizabeth Davies, who
has claimed the same, unless some other claimant shall sooner appear
and make out his claim thereto.
