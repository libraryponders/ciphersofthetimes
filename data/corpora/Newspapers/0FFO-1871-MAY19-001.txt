NOTICE to SHIPPERS.—A PENALTY of
TWENTY POUNDS attaches to all parties sending to the Lon¬
don, St. Katherine's or Victoria Docks, for shipment, GOODS of a COM¬
BUSTIBLE or otherwise of a dangerous NATURE, without their de¬
scription being distinctly marked on the tops of the packages conrain¬
ing the same. Sufficient information as to the nature of the goods
must also be contained in the shipping note ; and such articles will not
be received at the Docks until the export vessel is ready to take them
on board. Nitro-glycerine or glonine oil will not be received for ship¬
ment at the Docks under any circumstances.
JOHN G. GILKS, Superintendent.
London Docks, May, 1841.
