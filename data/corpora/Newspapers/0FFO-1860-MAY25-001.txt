PURIOUS RIFLE AMMUNITION. — Where as
certain RIFLE CARTRIDGES are now being advertised as "Dr.
Scoffern's," the same not being authorized or approved by me, I there¬
fore give notice, that no cartridges of mine are vended in commerce at
present, and that when vended none will be genuine that do not con¬
tain a bullet of full regulation guage, that do not withstand boiling
without wetting the charge, that are not already filled with powder,
and that are not sent out in packets of 10, the envelope of each packet
bearing my autograph signature.—JNO. SCOFFERN.
