BANK of ENGLAND.— Unclaimed Dividend.—Ap¬
plication having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of one dividend on the sum of £2,950
New £3 per Cent. Annuities, heretofore standing in the name of
ELIZABETH GREATWOOD TULL, of Charlton, Middlesex, widow,
and which dividend was paid over to the Commissioners for the Reduc¬
tion of the National Debt in consequence of its having remained un¬
clanned since the 5th April, 1859;—Notice is hereby given that, on the
expiration of three months from this date, the said Dividend will be
paid to Elizabeth Greatwood Tull, widow, who has claimed the same,
unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and make out his claim
thereto.
