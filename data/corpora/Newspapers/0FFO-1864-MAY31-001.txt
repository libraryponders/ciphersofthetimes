MIDDLESEX.—Notice is hereby given, that the
Committee of Justices of the Peace for this county, appointed
under the Act 15 and 16 Vic., g. 81, for the purpose of preparing a basis,
or standard, for fair and equal county rates in this county, such basis
or standard to be founded and prepared according to the provisions of
the said Act, have prepared and finally corrected and approved such
a basis or standard for a county rate in this county, and have laid the
same before the Court of General Sessions of the Peace for this county
holden next thereafter, and that such Court did thereupon order public
notice to be given as in the said Act directed; and notice is hereby
given, accordingly, that such basis or standard will be taken into con¬
sideration at the then and now next General Quarter Sessions of the
Peace, to be held in and for this county, to wit, on Thursday, the 14th
day of July next, at the Sessions-house, on Clerkenwell-green, at 12 at
noon; and such proceedings will then be had, with respect to the said
basis or standard, as are authorized by the Acts of Parlament in that
behalf, and shall seem meet. — Dated this 27th day of May, 1864.
C. HEATON ELLIS, Clerk of the Peace for Middlesex.
