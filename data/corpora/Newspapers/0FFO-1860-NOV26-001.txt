NOTICE.—Lioyd's, 15th November, 1860.—The
Committee for Managing the Affairs of Lloyd's hereby give
notice, that in consequence of a representation made by them to the
Trinity-house as to the desrability of faclitating the conveyance
of orders to masters of vessels expected to arrive in the Downs, the
Elder Brethren of that Corporation have been pleased to direct that
any NOTICES respecting orders awaiting the masters of vezsels ex¬
pected to arrive, which it is desired may be placed on board the pilot
