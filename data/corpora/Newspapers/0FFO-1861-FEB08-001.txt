CAUTION to the PUBLIC.—Whereas information
has been this day received from Switzerland and Hamburg, that
certain persons trading under the name and style of F. A. DODGSON
and SON, Newman-Street, Oxford-street, calling themselves general
brokers and merchants for the continent, have, by false and fraudulent
representations, obtained monies and bills of exchange to a large
amount, by pretending to be a legitimate company formed for the fol¬
lowing commercial purposes—viz, to advance money not for a longer
period than ten years, to make advances on landed property at 31 per
cent, per annum, with a certain sum to be deposited for the use of the
general manager for preliminary expenses; to provide for merchant
and others bills at two or three months date, to be domiciled by pay¬
ment of two per cent commission, to grant permission for contineal
firms to draw bills against remittances; to encash bills forwarded at a
commission of 1 percent, and to discount first class bills at two per
cent; and whereas the persons so falsely representing themselves as
aforesaid have ABSCONDED, merchants, bankers, tradesmen, and
others, who have been similarly defrauded, are requested to commu¬
nicate with Mr. Pollaky, Superintendent (foreign department), Private
Inquiry-office, 20, Devereaux-court, Temple; C. F. Field, Director.—
7th Feb., 1861.

EDICT. —The imp. Provincial Tribunal of Trieste
does hereby notify, that Anthony Werk, quondam Felix, by his
petition exhibited under date of the 4th inst. (June), No. 5,204, requested
the declaration of demise of Francis Urban Werk, quondam Felix, born
on the 1st of February, 1798, at Britof, in the Empire of Austria, who
in 1827 left direct for London, from whence nothing has been heard of
him since 1829 up to this day ; in deputing the advocate Dr. Denipoti¬
as guardian or curator of the said absent FRANCIS URBAN WERK,
the said Court SUMMONS the latter at the same time to appear
within a year before this tribunal, or else to authenticate his existence,
so much more as in the contrary proceedings would be entered into for
the declaration of his decease, and his property adjudicated and
handed over to that or those persons who will be lawfully entitled to it,
and particularly that part of it which is devolved to him in consequence
of the death of Cecilia Lucia Werk, widow of the late Francis Millauz.
—Trieste, the 27th June, 1860.
