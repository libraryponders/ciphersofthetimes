A COURT of MAYOR and ALDERMEN, holden in
the Inner Chamber of the Guildhall, of the city of London, on
Tuesday, the 19th day of November, 1872, and in the 36th year of the
reign of Victoria, of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ire¬
land Queen, &c.
Resolved unanimously, That the cordial hanks of this Court be pre¬
sented to the Right Honourable Sir Sils John Gibbons, Bart., late
Lord Mayor of this city, for the able manner in which he has discharged
the numerous and important duties of his high office during a period
rendered memorable by the state Visit of Her Majesty the Queen to
St. Paul's Cathedral, to offer thanks for the happy recovery of His
Royal Highness the Prince of Wales from his most dangerous illness,
on which occasion Her Majesty was graciously pleased, as a compli¬
ment to the city of London, to confer the dignity of Baronet upon the
Lord Mayor. This Court further wishes to record its entire satisfac¬
tion with the manner in which the late Lord Mayor has presided over
this Court and discharged his various magisterial duties; and also its
appreciation of the dignified and courteous manner in which he main¬
tamed the ancient hospitality of the city.	WOODTHORPE.
