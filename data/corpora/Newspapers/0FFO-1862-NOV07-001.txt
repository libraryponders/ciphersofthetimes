of England to direct the re-transfer from the Commissioners for the
Reduction of the National Debt of the sum of £161 1s. 6d. Consoli¬
dated £3 per Cent. Annuities, heretofore standing in the names of
Rev. CHARLES BARTER, of Cornworthy, Devon, JOHN LLOYD,
of Pound, Devon, Esq., and Sir WILLIAM ELFORD, Bart., of Bick¬
ham. Devon, and which was transferred to the said Commissioners

ferred, and the Dividends thereon paid, to the Rev. Charles Barter,
clerk, administrator with will annexed de bonis non of the Rev.
Charles Barter, deceased, who was the survivor, who has claimed the
same, unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and make out
his claim thereto.
