EXPLOSIVES ACT, 1875.—To. Gunmakers, lron¬
mongers, Oilmen, and other Persons Keeping Gunpower, Fire¬
works, Ammunition, Nitro-Glycerine, or other Explosive Substances
in the Metropolis.—The Metropolitan Board of Works, being the local
authority for the metropolis exclusive of the City of London, hereby
give notice, that on and after the 1st January, 1876, any person
desiring to keep any gunpowder or substance referred to in the Act
within the area of the Board's jurisdiction, must REGISTER his
PREMISES with the Board.
Premises may be registered either for the keeping of gunpowder, or
the keeping of other substances in the quantities prescribed by the
Act, and the Orders in Council, and applications for registration must
be made on the form prepared by the Board, and obtainable at their
office.
Premises will be registered on application being made in the proper
form and on payment.
fee of 1s, in money.

allowed by the Act in registered premises, may be obtained with the
