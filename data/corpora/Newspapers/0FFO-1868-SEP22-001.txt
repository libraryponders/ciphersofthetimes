PALESTINE EXPLORATION FUND.—
Patron—The QUEEN,
The objects of this Society are:—
1. The pursuit of archaological researches by means of excavations,
copying of inscriptions, drawing plans of ancient edifices and ruins,
taking accurate photographs of monuments, &c., in the Holy Land.
2. A topographical survey of the country, so as to fix localities and
distances, and furnish a correct description of the present aspect of

3. The geology, flora, fauna, and meteorology of the country.
4. The collection of authentic information, as to the manners,
toms, and traditious of the population of Palestine.
5. The formation of a Biblical Museum.
Reports of work done, plans of work, letters of Lieutenant Warren,
R.E., chief of the exploring party; the photographs of the Society, and
all other information can be obtained of the Secretary, by whom also
subscriptions are received, at the Society's offices, 9, Pall-mall East.
Subscriptions are also received at the Union Bank, Charing-cross
branch, 4. Pall-mall East: and at Messrs. Coutts and Co. Strand.
