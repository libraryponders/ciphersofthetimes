BANK of ENGLAND.— Unclaimed Dividends.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England, to direct the payment of 14 dividends on the sum of
£15 13s. 9d. per annum, Long Annuities, heretofore standing in the
name of WILLIAM HASLEDINE PEPYS, of the Poultry, F.R.S.,
and which dividends were paid over to the Commissioners for the Re¬
duction of the National Debt, in consequence of the first thereof hav¬
ing remained unclaimed since the 10th October, 1853;—Notice is hereby
given, that on the expiration of three months from this date, the said
Dividends will be paid to Robert Edward Pepys, one of the executors
of the said William Hasledine Pepys, deceased, who has claimed the
same, unless some other claimant shall sooner appear and make out his
claim thereto.
