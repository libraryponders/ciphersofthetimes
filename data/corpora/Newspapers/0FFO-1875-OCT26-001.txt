HAMILTON ANNE HAMILTON, now residing
at the Manor-house, Barkham, near Uckfield, in the county of
Sussex, and Graduate of Trinity College, Cambridge, do hereby give
notice that as, and from the date hereof, it is my intention to assume
my family name of Douglas, and prefix the said name of Douglas to
my surname of Hamilton, and it is my intention to ADOPT and USE
the SURNAME of DOUGLAS-HAMILTON only in all future deeds,
instruments, and documents whatsoever, and in all actions, suits, and
proceedings whatsoever, to which or in which I may be a party or other¬
wise interested, and for every other purpose, and on any other occa¬
sion whatsoever, and I do hereby expressly authorise and desire every
person whomsoever to designate and describe me by such surname of
Douglas-Hamilton. As witness my hand this 20th day of October,
1875. HAMILTON HAMILTON. Witness—A. Tingcombe.
