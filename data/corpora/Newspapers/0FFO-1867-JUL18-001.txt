LOTICE is hereby given that I, the undersigned
HENRY FRASER JAMES COAPE ARNOLD, of Mirables, in
the Isle of Wight, have ASSUMED the SURNAME of ARNOLD
(being the family name of my maternal ancestors), in addition to that
of Coape, and by a deed poll under my hand and seal, executed on this
day, which is intended to be forthwith enrolled in the High Court of
Chancery, I have declared my intention from thenceforth to assume
the surname of Arnold in addition to and after the surname of Coape.
and in all legal and other documents to adopt and use the names of
Henry Fraser James Coape Arnold, and as far as may be to be known
and designated on all occasions as Henry Fraser James Coape Arnold.—
Dated this 16th day of July, 18
