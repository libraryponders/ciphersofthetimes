EGYPT, the Nile, Suer Canal, and Syra.—in conse¬
quence of the limited hotel accommodation in Egypt, and the
exorbitant charges which the hotels have resolved to make this season,
the EGYPTIAN AGENCY have determined to enter into no more
contracts to send travellers to the opening of the Suez Canal.
Boats and dragomen for the Nile trip can still be hired at the Agency
in London, and travellers can be taken to the Suez Canal after they
return from Upper Egypt.
Apply to the Manager of the Buckingham Palace Hotel, Bucking¬
ham-gate ; or to the Egyptian Agency, 34, Nicholas-lane, city.
