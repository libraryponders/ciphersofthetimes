CAUTION. — SCHWEITZER's COCOATINA.—
Whereas Edward Hunter Atkins and Otto Schweitzer, carrying
on business under the firm of Otto Schweitzer, Atkins, and Co., have
lately offered for sale a preparation called "Cacaotine," or
"Cacaoine," packed in cases of a similar description to those used by
us, and have otherwise attempted to induce the public to purchase an
inferior article instead of our cocoatina; Notice is hereby given that,
in a suit instituted by us in the Court of Chancery, Vice-Chancellor
Sir Richard Malins has granted an INJUNCTION, restraining the
said Edward Hunter Atkins and Otto Schweitzer, their servants,
agents, and workmen, from selling, or offering for sale, or causing or
procuring to be sold or offered for sale, any preparation of cocoa or
other article under the name of cocoatina, or Schweitzer's cocoatina,
or under any name or description similar to, or only colourably differ¬
ing from the name or description of "Cocoatina," or "Schweitzer's
Cocoatina," and from using or employing, or causing or permitting to
be used or employed, any label, notice, or placard containing the name
or words "Cocoatina" or "Schweitzer's Cocoatina," or any name or
words similar thereto, or only colourably differing therefrom, as ap¬
plicable to any preparation or article of manufacture other than our
manufacture ; and all persons are hereby cautioned against selling, or
offering for saie, or purchasing any preparation of cocoa or other
article (not being our manufacture) under the name of cocoatina, or
under any name similar thereto. —Dated this 20th day of July, 1868.
