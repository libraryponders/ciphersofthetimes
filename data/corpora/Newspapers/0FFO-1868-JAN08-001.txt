DOG LICENCES.—The Commissioners of Inland
Revenue give notice that all Dog Licences granted in the year
1867 expired on the 31st December last, and that every person keeping
a dog in the current year is now required to take out a new licence, the
duty payable for each dog kept being 5s.
The penalty for keeping a dog without licence is £5.
