Co.'s VIENNA PRIZE WIRE NETTING seem to have induced cer¬
tain dealers in wire netting to advertise an inferior article at reduced
prices, specifying the mesh, gauge, &c., as if such were the same in
quality and value as the Prize Wire Netting of which Messrs. J. B.

thickness or strength of wire, the weight of the rolls and the exact
measurements, the general fmish of the manufacture, and the quaiity
of the galvanizing. This examination will prove the respective quali¬
ties of the netting.—90, Cannon-street, London, E.C.
