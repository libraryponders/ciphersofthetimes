CHANGE of SURNAME.—Topham, Haynes.—
Notice is hereby given, that I, John Robert Topham, of Wensor
Castle. Deeping-common, in the parish of West Deeping, in the county
of Lincoln, farmer and grazier, have, pursuant to a proviso contained in
the will of James Haynes, late of Wensor Castle aforesaid, Esquire
deceased, as from the 18th day of January, 1869, ADOPTED and TAKEN
the SURNAME of HAYNES, in addition to that of Topham, so that
henceforth I may for all purposes use and bear the surname of Haynes,
and be called and known, and on all occasions be described, by the
name of John Robert Topham Haynes, instead of John Robert Top¬
ham and I have by a deed poll under my handjand seal, bearing date
the 18th day of January, 1869, and duly enrolled in the High Court of
Chancery, declared such adoption of the said surname of Haynes, as is
more fully expressed in the said deed poll.—Dated this 25th day of
January, 1869.
JOHN ROBERT TOPHAM.
