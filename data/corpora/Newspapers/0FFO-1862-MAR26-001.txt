NOTICE to PARENTS and GUARDIANS.—A
BOY, about 11 years of age, whe gives the name of GEORGE
BAKER, was FOUND at SWINDON, Wilts, on the 4th day of March
instant, in a state of destitution, and taken to the Workhouse of the
Highworth and Swindon Union. He states that his father was coach¬
man to Mr. Williams, of 3, Devonshire-terrace, Bayswater, and died
there in February last. That he was making his way to Bath, where
he says his father was legally settled. The boy has dark brown hair
and eyes, and when found was dressed in dark cloth clothes. He is an
intelligent lad, and answers freely all questions put to him. Informe
tion to be given to Mr. Haynes, Superintendent of Police, or Mr. E. F.
Avenell, relieving officer, Swindon, Wilts.
