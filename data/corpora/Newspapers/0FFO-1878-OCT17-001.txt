DESCENDANTS of GEORGE SMITH, deceased.—
George Smith, until 1837 a Gunner in the 1st Troop, 1st Brigade,
Royal Horse Artillery, at Mecrut, then Quartermaster-Sergeant,
Kemaon Battalion, at Almorah, afterwards assistant to an indigo
planter in Bengal, died in the General Hospital, Calcutta, in 1841 or
1842. He is supposed to have married at Almorah about 1837, and to
have had two children only—daughters. His widow is supposed to
have married a sergeant at Cawnpore or Lucknow and the daughters
to have been killed in the Mutiny. Anyone supplying to the under¬
signed authentic EVIDENCE of the DEATH or FAILURE of DE¬
SCENDANTS of the said George Smith will be REWARDED, and
any descendants living of the said George Smith will hear of some¬
thing to their advantage on applying to Isaac Cooke and Sons, Solici¬
tors, Bristol, England.
