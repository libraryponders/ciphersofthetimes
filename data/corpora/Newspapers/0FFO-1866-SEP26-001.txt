PARISH of CRANFORD, in the county of Middle¬
sex.—Notice is hereby given, that at a VESTRY MEETING
held in the parish church of Cranford aforesaid, on the 24th day of
September, 1866, and convened for the purpose after 21 clear days'
notice, a RESOLUTION was passed by a majority:—
"That the said parish of Cranford be, and is hereby, formed into a
Special Drainage District, for the purpose of the Sewage Utilization
Act, 1865, in pursuance of the Sanitary Act, 1866."
EDW. WILSON, Hon. Secretary.
Dated Cranford, September 24 1866.
