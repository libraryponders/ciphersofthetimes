ESSAY on KLEPTOMANIA, with a view to determine whether

stances this mischievous propensity becomes criminal. The successful
Essayist must indicate all the best known characteristics of Klepto¬
maniacs, and give an outline of the type of cerebral organisation

David Wilson, Brook-street, Grosvenor-square. American and Foreign
papers will please to notify that this is an open prize.
