Notice.—Whereas several steam and other vessels have, within the
last few months, by the culpable negligence of the persons in com¬
mand, come into collision with various light vessels belonging to this
Corporation, and particularly with the Newarp Light Ship, stationed
off Winterton, in the county of Norfolk, doing considerable damage to
the light ships, and endangering the navigation of the coast for the
time by other vessels, notice is hereby given that in every case of a
vessel coming into collision with the Newarp Light Ship, or any other
light ship, in future, by the negligence or carelessness of any person in
command, the Corporation of Trinity-house will, in addition to insti¬
tuting proceedings against the owners for the recovery of any damages
occasioned, prosecute the person or persons through whose carelessness
