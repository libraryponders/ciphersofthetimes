THE late FATAL FIRE at the WEST-END.—
Messrs. PEARCE and SON beg to inform their numerous
patrons that they are enabled to CARRY ON their BUSINESS at
No. 17, Conduit-street, as usual.
MESSRS. W. WILKINS and Co., of 24 and 25,
Long-acre, Lighthouse Manufacturers and Oil Merchants,
whose premises were partly destroyed by FIRE on the 13th inst., wish
to state, for the information of their friends and the public generally,
that, so far as they have been able to ascertain, the fire originated
either in the second or third floors, and not in the oil collars, as an¬
nounced in some of the papers. The cause of the fire is at present
unknown. There was no explosion of casks of oil, there being nothing
of what is termed explosive oils in the cellars. The BUSINESS will
be CARRIED ON, for the present, in the same premises, without
interruption.
