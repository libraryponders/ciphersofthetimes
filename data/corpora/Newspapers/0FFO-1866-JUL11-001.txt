NOTICE IMPORTANT.—WANTED, any OLD
PAPERS or DOCUMENTS containing information or intelli¬
gence of ROBERT JENINGS, the rebel General who fought under
the standard of Prince Charles Edward in the Rebellion of 1745-6. He
married a Dutch heiress from Whitehall in Holland about 1742. She
came in search of him to England in 1749, and found him near Leeds.
After this time they lived secluded. She was repeatedly advertised
for by her friends. That advertisement is now particularly wanted
to complete a pedigree ; or any old letters of correspondence between
these parties might materially assist in this matter. Any person hold¬
ing these papers may find it to their own advantage to communicate
with R. W. Read. Prospect-villa, Dodworth-road, Barnsley, Yorkshire.

day of June, 1866. I wholly abandoned and discontinued the surname
of Hipwell and ASSUMED, took, and adopted the SURNAME of
