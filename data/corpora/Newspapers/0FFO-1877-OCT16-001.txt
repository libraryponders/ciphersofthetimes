borne Chase and New Forest Turnpike are now winding up the affairs
of the said Trust, notice is hereby given, that all persons having any
CLAIM or DEMAND upon or against the said Trustees are re¬
quested to send to me, the undersigned, at Fordingbridge, in the
County of Southampton, particulars, in writing, of their claims or de¬
mands, on or before Monday, the 31st day of December, 1877, after

day of October, 1877.	H. S. DAVY, Clerk to the said Trustees.
