THE Secretary of State for India publishes, for
general information, the following notification, issued by the
Government of Bombay:—
General Department.
Notice is hereby given, that sealed TENDERS will be received, up
to 24 hours after the arrival in Bombay of the first mail in June next,
by the Postmaster-General of Bombay, at his office in Bombay, for the
performance of either of the following MAIL SERVICES:—
1. Mail service between Bombay and Kurrachee, and vice versa,
twice a month, in connexion with the arrival and departure of the
overland mails, to commence on or after the 1st July, 1862.
. 2. Mail service, eight times in the year, between Bombay and Persian
Gulf, via Kurrachee, and vice versa.
Tenders will also be received for the joint performance of both the
above mentioned services.
The contract will be for a period of five years, with option of
termination previous to its expiration, either by Government or by the
company whose tender may be accepted, on giving 12 months notice.
The tenderers should state the number, tonnage, and horse-power
of the steamers they will employ for the above services, and the rate at
which they will bind themselves to perform them.
Further particulars may be ascertained on inquiry at the office of
