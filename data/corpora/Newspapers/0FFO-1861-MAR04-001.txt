The Examination will be of two grades (1st and 2d), and will be
pen to all persons, without limitation as to age, but students in schools
of art, teachers and pupil teachers of publie schools under inspection
of Privy Council, are ineligible for examination in the first or easier
grade.

models, ditto memory, practical geometry, perspective, mechanical
drawing.
Prizes will be given to candidates whose drawings reach the required
standare.
