BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividend.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of One Dividend on the sum of
£1,500 Reduced 3 per cent. Annuities, heretofore standing in the
names of GEORGE SELBY, of Serjeant's-inn, Fleet-street, London,
gentleman, and GEORGE WALKER CUMING, of High-Street,
Portsmouth, Hants, Esq., and which dividend was paid over to the
Commissioners for the Reduction of the National Debt, in conse¬
quence of its having remained unclaimed since the 5th April, 1841;—
Notice is hereby given, that, on the expiration of three months from
this date, the said dividend will be paid to the said George Selby
and George Walker Cuming, who have claimed the same, unless some
other claimant shall sooner appear and make out his claim thereto.
