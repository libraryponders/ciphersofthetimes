having remained unclaimed since the 10th October, 1851;—Notice is

said Edward Barnes, deceased, who has claimed the same, unless some
other claimant shall sooner appear and make out his claim thereto.

THE ARCHITECTURAL EXHIBITION, 1862.—
The Committee beg to announce that the EXHIBITION will
OPEN as usual next year. All works to be sent to 9, Conduit-street, on

the art in Great Britain.
