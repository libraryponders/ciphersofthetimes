Sand, came into COLLISION with the EAST GOODWIN LIGHT

doing considerable damage to the light ship, extinguishing several of
the lights, and thereby endangering the safety of the vessel, the liveg
of the crew, and the navigation of the coast by other vessels. And
whereas the name of the brig, or of the master in command, could not

oration of Trinity-house, of London, as to the NAME of the
VESSEL, and her owners, and the master in command at the time,

master and owners, or either of tnem, under the provisions of the
Merchant Shipping Acts. Information to be forwarded to the under¬
signed.
