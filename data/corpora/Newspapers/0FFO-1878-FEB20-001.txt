The objects for which the Association is proposed to be established
are:—
The acquiring of moneys, funds, and estates, both real and personal,
by gift, subscription, devise, bequest, purchase, or otherwise (but as
regards lands, and property savouring of realty, so far only as the
Association lawfully may acquire the same by virtue of the Licence of
the Board of Trade or otherwise), and the appropriation and distripu¬
tion of the same, and of the income arising therefrom, or from the
sale or conversion thereof, in all or some or one of the ways, or for all
or some or one of the purposes hereinafter mentioned, that is to sty,
to and amongst the Church of England Temperance Society, within
the respective Provinces of Canterbury and York, for the time being,
but especially for and towards establishing, within the said Provinces
of Canterbury and York, respectivery, a Church of England Temper¬
ance Sick and Burial Society, with branches in connexion with the
same, in all the parishes in she various dioceses of the said Provinces
of Canterbury and York, when such last naned Society is duly regis¬
tered in conformity with the provisions of the Friendly Societies Act.
and, generally, in providing for the maintenance and furtherance of
the work of the Church of England Temperance Society, and the
Church of England Temperance Sick and Burial Society when esta¬
blished, and doing all such things as are incidental or conducive to
the attainment of the above objects, or any of them.
Notice is hereby furtner given, that any person, company, or corpo¬
