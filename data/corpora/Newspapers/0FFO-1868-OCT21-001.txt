MESSRS, MOXON and Co., Publishers, London.—
Gentlemen.—We regret very much that in "A Selection of
Readings," &c., compiled by one of us, and lately published by the
other, two poems by the late Mr. Hood, and two poems by Mr. Tenny¬
son, of which the copyright belongs to you, have been included.
The invasion of your copyright was made by us inadvertently, and
we now APOLOGISE for it. We have given up to you all the remain¬

incurred by you. You are at full liberty to publish this apology at our
expense. — We are, Gentlemen, your most obedient servants.
