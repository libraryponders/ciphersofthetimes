MPORTANT NOTICE—SWEARS and WELLS
greatly regret to inform their patrons, specially those residing
abroad, that having discovered a large quantity of letters secreted
on their premises, the majority of them being answers to inquiries
from and directed to their customers, and having reason to be¬
lieve that more have been destroyed and the postage stamps in every
case dishonestly appropriated, they hasten to make this public an¬
nouncement of the fact, in order to remove from the minds of the
numerous customers who have been inconvenienced by this untoward
afair, the unfavourable impression that the correspondence arrange¬
ments of the establishment are negligently conducted. The Lilliputiar
