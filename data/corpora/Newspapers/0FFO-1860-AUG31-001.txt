Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the re-transfer from the Commissioners for the Re¬
duction of the National Debt of the sum of £55 Consolidated Three
per Cent. Annuities, heretofore standing in the names of JONATHAN
CRANE, of Bromley, St. Leonard's, Middlesex, gentleman, ROBERT
HANBURY, of Christ Church, Middlesex, brewer, and JOHN

Hanbury, the survivor, who has claimed the same, unless some other
claimant shall sooner appear and make out his claim thereto.
