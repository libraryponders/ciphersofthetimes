PATENT FILTER PRESSES.—I, James Oxley,
of Frome, in the county of Somerset, brewers' engineer, hereby
give notice that I OBTAINED from Her Majesty's Solicitor-General,
on the 18th day of March instant, LETTERS PATENT under the
Great Seal for my invention of machines for separating liquids from
semi-fluids and substances, although my application was opposed by
the solicitor and counsel for Messrs. Needham and Kite, of Vauxhall,
London, who represented my invention to be an infringement of their
rights; and I further give notice that I am making and selling such

allowed to refer, and that I am prepared to indemnify any purchaser
against loss or liability to be incurred by reason of his purchasing or
using any machine of my invention,	JAMES OXLEY.
