TUESDAY, the 20th day of October, 1863, and in
the 27th year of the reign of Victoria, of the United Kingdom
of Great Britain and Ireland, Queen, &c.
Resolved unanimously, —That this Court receive with very great
regret the intelligence of the death of their much-respected brother
John Humphery, Esq., and remembering the long peried during
which he devoted his valuable services for the benefit of his fellow
citizens, and the exemplary manner in which he fulfilled the duties
of the high and important public offices connected with this city, have
a melancholy satisfaction in placing on record this testimony to his
worth, and in expressing their sincere condolence in the painful
bereavement which has been sustained by his family and friends,
WOODTHORPE.
