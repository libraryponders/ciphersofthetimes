FORGET-ME-NOT. —Letter received safely again.
Cannot I write anywhere to you to assure you of my devotion?
It is so hard not to be able to write. When are you coming back?
 long so for you. No one can fill your place, and life is a void in your
absence. All is forgiven, so think no more of what was written at
first. I know now you could not return as I had hoped.—Always your
very own.

miles from the English Coast, betwixt 5 and 6 a.m. on the 6th of

his family. Communications can be addressed to Mr. J. L. Milner,
