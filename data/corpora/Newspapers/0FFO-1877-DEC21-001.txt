INFORMATION WANTED.—The friends of a young
Armenian Officer, Captain MICHAEL TOMAEFF, who was in
the Rossian service, wounded and taken prisoner by the Turks when
they recaptured Fort Ajizie, near Erzeroum, are anxious to know his
whereabouts. If alive, he is supposed to be in Erzeroum, Trebisonde,
or Constanticople. His family would be very thankful if any one pos¬
sessing the information would kindly COMMUNICATE it to Mile.
Smirnoff, Hotel d'Albe Avenue d'Alma, Paris.
