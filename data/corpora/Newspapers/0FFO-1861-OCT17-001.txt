This gentleman, in 1849, carried on the business of a brush
manufacturer, in partnership with one James Thompson, under the
firm of Thompson and Winter, at West-street, Reading. His daughter
Elizabeth intermarried with the said James Thompson (who died in
August, 1849), and, it is believed, Mr. Winter continued to reside with
his daughter at two different residences in Reading till the year 1852,
when they both quitted. Any one who can give INFORMATION to
Messrs. Spyer and Son, of 8, Broad-street-buildings, London, whether
Mr. Winter is alive or dead (if alive, where he is residing; if dead,
where he was buried), will be rewarded for his or her trouble.
