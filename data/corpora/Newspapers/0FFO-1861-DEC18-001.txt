MUSIC HALLS and PUBLIC GARDENS.—At
a MEETING of the Proprietors of Music Halls and Public
was resolved, That in consequence of the melancholy event of the
death of His Royal Highness the Prince Consort, the Music Halls and
Public Gardens, in the metropolis, should be closed this evening (Mon¬
day), and also on the day of the funeral.
And it was further resolved, That the Meeting should reed
the proprietors of Music Halls and Public Gardens throughout the
United Kingdom to close on the last-mentioned day.
These resolutions to be inserted in The Times and other daily papers.
