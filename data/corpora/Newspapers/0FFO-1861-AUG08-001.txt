THE COUNTY of LANCASTER RIFLE CON¬
TEST.—The COUNTY of LANCASTER RIFLE ASSOCIA¬
TION will hold their FIRST ANNUAL PRIZE MEETING at
Altear (nine miles from Liverpool, on the Liverpool and Southport
Railway), on Monday, the 26th of August, and following days. Prizes
to the amount of about £1,000 are offered for competition to the line,
militia, yeomanry and volunteers of the county, and all comers. There
will be pool targets open to all. The entries close on Monday, the 12th
of August. For particulars apply to the Assistant-Secretary, County
of Lancaster Rifle Association, Preston.—Preston, July, 1861.
