hereby given, that a new Consular Tariff has been promulgated
in the Madrin Gazette of the 25th of July last, and that the REGU¬
LAIIONS respecting the CLEARANCE of VESSELS for ports in
Spai have been sitered. Shippers will be required to present at the
Spanisn Consulates duplicate invoices of all goods shipped by them.
Tnese involces may be in Spanish or Engtish for the present. The
shippers wil have to pay at the Consulates a fee of 10d. for each
case or parcel shippol, excepting certain goods in buik and bags,
on which the charses will be 16s. 8d. for the first 50 tons and under,

will have to deliver at the Consulates a copy of the bill of lading cor¬
respouding to each invoice. These regulations will be enforced from
and after Monday, the 17th instant. Full particulars will be ex¬
plained to parties interested on personal application at this office.
