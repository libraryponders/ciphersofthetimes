FINE ART.—Preliminary Advertisement.—Mr. J.
C. MOORE begs to inform the profession and his friends that, in
consequence of the actions at law now pending between himself and
the Dean of Christ Church, Oxford, with reference to architectural
questions, he is now devoting his time to FINE ART; and he is pre¬
pared to afford such information to the profession as may be required
of him; and it is proposed that the arrangements as to terms should
be through the medium of the Secretary of the Art Union.
