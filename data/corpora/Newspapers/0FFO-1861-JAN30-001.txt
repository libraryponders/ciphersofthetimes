BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividends.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of two dividends on the sum of
£1,343 6s. Consolidated Three per Cent. Annuities, heretofore standing
in the names of Rev. WALTER SCOTT COWARD, of Spanish¬
town, Jamaica, and ANNE EMILY COWARD, his wife, both
deceased, and which dividends were paid over to the Commissioners for
the Reduction of the National Debt, in consequence of the first thereof
having remained unclaimed since the 5th of January, 1839;—Notice
is hereby given that, on the expiration of three months from this
date, the said Dividends will be paid to Walter Scott Vidal Coward,
administrator of Rev. Walter Scott Coward, deceased, who was the
survivor, who has claimed the same, unless some other claimant shall
sooner appear and make out his claim thereto.
