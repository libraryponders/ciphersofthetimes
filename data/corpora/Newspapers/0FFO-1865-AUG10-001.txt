NATIONAL ASSOCIATION for the PREVEN
TION Of CATTLE DISEASES.—This Association has bee
formed to meet the serious difficulties which have arisen from the in
portation of the Russian Cattle Plague, and to advocate the cause
the sufferers by this disease.
The Provisional Committee earnestly request immediate commun
cations from persons anxious to co-operate in averting a great nationd
calamity, and they especially seck the earllest information of the a
pearance of the disease and of its extent, as it is only by a perfect kno
ledge of the real progress of the malady that any efficient means e.
be made available.
Any information received from persons whose stock may be affecte
or otherwise shall be regarded as strictly private and confidential, an
every effort will be made on the part of the Committee to adon
means whereby the malady may he confined within the narrowe
possible limits.
A subscription of one guinea annually qualifies for membership.
All willing to co-operate in this important undertaking are requeste
to address letters and personal inquiries to
