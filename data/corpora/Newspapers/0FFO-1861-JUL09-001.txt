BANK of ENGLAND.—Unclaimed Dividends.—
Application having been made to the Governors of the Bank of
England to direct the payment of Two Dividends on the Sum of
£722 Reduced Three per Cent. Annuities, heretofore standing in the
name of SUSANNA BAKER, of Fore-street, Cripplegate, widow, de¬
ceased, and which dividends were paid over to the Commissioners for
the Reduction of the National Debt, in consequence of the first thereof
having remained unclaimed since the 10th of October, 1828;—Notice
is hereby given that, on the expiration of three months from this
date, the said Dividends will be paid to the Rev. Thomas James, the
surviving executor, who has claimed the same, unless some other
claimant shall sooner appear and make out his claim thereto.
